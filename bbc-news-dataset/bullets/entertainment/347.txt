- Mike Leigh's Vera Drake has received seven nominations at the British Independent Film Awards.
- Shane Meadows' Dead Man's Shoes has received eight nominations.
- Vera Drake and Dead Man's Shoes are in the running for best film, along with My Summer of Love, Touching the Void and Shaun of the Dead.
- Shane Meadows and Kevin McDonald have received best director nominations.
- Paddy Considine is nominated for best actor for Dead Man's Shoes.
- Considine is also nominated for a best supporting actor award for My Summer of Love.
