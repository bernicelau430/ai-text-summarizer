- The Royal Academy of Arts held its annual Secrets sale.
- Money raised will go to the RCA's Fine Art Student Award Fund.
- Artists like Damien Hirst and Tracey Emin designed postcards.
- Film director Ken Loach, fashion designer Hussein Chalayan, and former Blur guitarist Graham Coxon also designed postcards.
- There was a big queue at the start of the sale.
- People spent time studying each postcard.
