- Saul Israel Holiff, the former manager of Johnny Cash, has died at the age of 79.
- Mr Holiff, who was also a concert promoter, managed Cash's career between 1960 and 1973.
- The Canadian music manager also managed Tommy Hunter and the Statler Brothers.
- An entrepreneur from an early age, Mr Holiff served as a rear air gunner in the Royal Canadian Air Force during World War II, afterwards setting up a drive-in restaurant.
