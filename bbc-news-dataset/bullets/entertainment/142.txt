- The Bravery topped the BBC News website's Sound of 2005 poll
- They are an electro-rock group from New York
- Sam Endicott, the singer, expressed feeling "great" about the win
- The band was described as having "great guitars and a mastery of the electro-clash sound"
- Bloc Party came in second and UK garage MC Kano was third
- The Bravery were compared to The Cure and New Order
