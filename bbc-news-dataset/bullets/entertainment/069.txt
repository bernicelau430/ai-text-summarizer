- Jamie Foxx and Hilary Swank have won the Screen Actors Guild Awards for best male and female film actors.
- Swank triumphed for playing a gutsy female boxer in Million Dollar Baby.
- Both Foxx and Swank are now considered to be among the favorites to get Oscars.
- Morgan Freeman took the best supporting actor award for playing a prize-fighter turned gym manager in Million Dollar Baby.
- Modest wine country comedy Sideways knocked out favourites Million Dollar Baby and The Aviator by taking the top prize for best cast performance.
