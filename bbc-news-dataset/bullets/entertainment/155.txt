- A Nirvana box set featuring 68 unreleased tracks has gone on sale in the US
- The release follows years of legal wrangles
- Courtney Love, Kurt Cobain's widow, initially blocked the release
- Love did not want the unreleased song You Know You're Right on the set
- The legal fight centered on the studio recording of You Know You're Right
- The song is considered one of Nirvana's unreleased gems
- Cobain biographer Charles Cross described it as Nirvana unedited
