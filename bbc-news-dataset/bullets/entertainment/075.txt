- Mike Leigh's Vera Drake has seven nominations at the British Independent Film Awards.
- Shane Meadows' Dead Man's Shoes received eight nominations.
- Vera Drake, Dead Man's Shoes, My Summer of Love, Touching the Void and Shaun of the Dead are running for best film.
- Shane Meadows and Kevin McDonald are nominated for best director.
- Paddy Considine is nominated for best actor for Dead Man's Shoes.
- Considine is also nominated for best supporting actor for My Summer of Love.
- Also in the running for a clutch of awards are My Summer of Love and Enduring Love.
