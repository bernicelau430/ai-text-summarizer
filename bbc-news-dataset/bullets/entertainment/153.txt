- OutKast won three prizes at the MTV Europe Music Awards in Rome
- They won best group, best video, and best song for Hey Ya!
- Usher won best male and best album for Confessions
- Muse won best alternative act and best British artists
- D12 won best hip-hop
- Anastacia won best pop act
- Britney Spears was named best female
- OutKast previously won four US MTV Awards and three Grammys
- Linkin Park won best rock band
