- Comedy Meet The Fockers has held on to the number one spot at the North American box office for a second week.
- It took $42.8m at the weekend, making its overall takings more than $163m in 12 days.
- The success of Meet the Fockers could help produce record box office revenue for 2004.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% on 2003's figures - which was blamed on Christmas falling over a weekend this year.
