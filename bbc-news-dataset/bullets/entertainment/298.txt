- Spider-Man creator Stan Lee won a court battle with Marvel and is to get a multi-million dollar windfall.
- Lee should get a tenth of profits generated since November 1998 by Marvel TV and movie productions.
- The New York court did not rule on Lee's claims to a share of profits from Spider-Man and Hulk movie merchandise, which will be decided at a future trial.
- Lee took legal action in 2002, saying Marvel shut him out of profits from the first blockbuster film.
