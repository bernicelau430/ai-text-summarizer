- Robert De Niro has returned to the top of the North American box office with his film Hide and Seek.
- Last week's top film, Ice Cube's Are We There Yet?, slipped to second place with $17m.
- Million Dollar Baby came in at number three during its first weekend of wide release.
- The Aviator was at number six for the weekend with $7.5m.
- Coach Carter fell two places to number four, taking $8m in its third week.
- De Niro recently spent three weeks at the top with comedy Meet The Fockers, which was at number five this week.
- Oscar best-picture nominee Sideways entered the top ten for the first time in its 15th week of release.
