- Mira Nair directed the film version of William Makepeace Thackeray's novel Vanity Fair.
- Nair stated that the book has been a favorite through her life.
- Nair cast Reese Witherspoon as Becky Sharp.
- Nair's film picks up on how Thackeray, who was born in India but moved to England as a young man, viewed the world.
- Nair stated that she jumped at the chance to work on the film, which she made in a Bollywood style.
- Nair also defended her decision to cast an American actress because it helped the film.
- The film has a new ending, with Becky Sharp running off for a new life in India.
