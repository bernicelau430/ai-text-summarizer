- The Sundance Film Festival will feature two new international cinema competitions during its January season in Utah.
- International films will have the same prominence as US films.
- The theme of war dominates the movies being showcased.
- Twelve films competing in the world cinema documentary category focus on countries and people under siege.
- The world cinema dramatic category includes works from Germany, South Korea, Angola, China, Denmark, and Australia.
- Forty-two films will debut at Sundance, including The Liberace of Baghdad by British director Sean McAllister.
