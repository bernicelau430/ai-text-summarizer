- Film star Robert De Niro has returned to the top of the North American box office with his film Hide and Seek.
- Last week's top film, Ice Cube's road-trip comedy Are We There Yet?, slipped to second place with $17m (£9m).
- Clint Eastwood's Million Dollar Baby led the Oscar hopefuls with $11.8m (£6.3m), coming in at number three during its first weekend of wide release.
- De Niro recently spent three weeks at the top with comedy Meet The Fockers, which was at number five this week.
