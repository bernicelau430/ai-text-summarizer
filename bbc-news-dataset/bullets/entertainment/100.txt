- Meet The Fockers held on to number one spot at North American box office for a second week.
- Took $42.8m at the weekend, making its overall takings more than $163m in 12 days.
- Took $19.1m on Christmas Day, the highest takings on that day in box office history.
- Broke box office records for money taken on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% on 2003's figures.
- Success of Meet the Fockers could help produce record box office revenue for 2004.
