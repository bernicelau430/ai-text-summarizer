- Meet The Fockers remained the number one film at the North American box office for a second week.
- The movie earned $42.8m (£23.7m) at the weekend.
- Total takings exceeded $163m (£90m) in 12 days.
- Meet the Fockers broke box office records for New Year's Eve ($12.2m) and New Year's Day ($18m).
- It also took $19.1m (£9.9m) on Christmas Day.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% from 2003, due to Christmas falling on a weekend.
- The success of Meet the Fockers could lead to record box office revenue for 2004.
