- The Last Samurai was the biggest foreign movie hit in Japan last year, taking 13.8bn yen (£70.7m).
- Japanese films accounted for 37.5% of Japan's box office total last year.
- Foreign films taking the remaining 62.5%.
- Animated movie Howl's Moving Castle took 20bn yen (£102m) to become the biggest film in Japan in 2004.
- Japan's box office received a 3.8% boost last year.
- Sales of movies on DVD and video amounted to 497bn yen (£2.54bn) for the year.
