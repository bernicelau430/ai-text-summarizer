- Stage production of The Sound of Music is to be performed for first time in Austrian capital, 40 years after film was released
- Film was never shown in any cinema in Austria and was not broadcast on television until early 1990s
- Sensitivities about Nazism during wartime Austria and issues towards von Trapp family themselves could explain reluctance to embrace the musical
- Musical is based on true story of the von Trapp family who formed a singing troupe and escaped from Nazi-occupied Austria in 1938
