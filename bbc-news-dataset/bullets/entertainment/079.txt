- Russian drama The Return has been named winner of the BBC Four World Cinema Award.
- The Return previously won the 2003 Golden Lion award at the Venice Film Festival.
- Ross was also involved in the deliberations.
- The winner was chosen by a panel which included Gillian Anderson, critic Roger Clarke and Touching the Void director Kevin McDonald.
- The winner of the World Cinema Award last year was the French animated feature Belleville Rendezvous.
