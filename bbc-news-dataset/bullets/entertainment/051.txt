- Mike Leigh's award-winning abortion drama Vera Drake has scooped seven nominations at British Independent Film Awards
- Venice winner faces stiff competition from Shane Meadows' critically acclaimed Dead Man's Shoes
- Also in running for a clutch of awards are My Summer of Love and stalker drama Enduring Love
- Considine is also nominated for best supporting actor award for My Summer of Love
- Joining Vera Drake and Dead Man's Shoes in running for best film are My Summer of Love, climbing documentary Touching the Void and zombie comedy Shaun of the Dead
- Meadows and Kevin McDonald won best director nominations
