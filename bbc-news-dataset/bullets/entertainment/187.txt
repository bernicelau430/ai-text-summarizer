- New York band Scissor Sisters won a gig of the year award for their V Festival performance.
- The award was voted for by listeners of Virgin Radio.
- The top 10 was dominated by newcomers on the music scene.
- Glasgow's Franz Ferdinand earned a place at number nine for their performance in Glasgow.
- Oxfam's Make Fair Trade gig at London's Hammersmith Apollo was at number seven, featuring performances by REM, Razorlight, and Coldplay's Chris Martin.
