- Comedy Meet The Fockers held the number one spot at the North American box office for a second week.
- It set records for New Year's Eve and New Year's Day takings.
- It took $19.1m (£9.9m) on Christmas Day alone, a record.
- It took $42.8m (£23.7m) at the weekend, making its overall takings more than $163m (£90m) in 12 days.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% on 2003.
- The success of Meet the Fockers could help produce record box office revenue for 2004.
