- Ocean's Twelve, starring George Clooney, Brad Pitt, and Julia Roberts, debuted at number one in the US box office.
- The sequel made slightly more than Ocean's Eleven in its opening weekend.
- Steven Soderbergh directed Ocean's Eleven and returned to direct Ocean's Twelve.
- Ocean's Twelve had the fourth-biggest opening for a December release in the US, following the three Lord of the Rings films.
- A New York Times review called the sequel "unabashedly trivial".
