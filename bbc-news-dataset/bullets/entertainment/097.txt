- Michael Moore's Fahrenheit 9/11 won best film at the US People's Choice Awards.
- Mel Gibson's The Passion of the Christ won best drama.
- Johnny Depp was favourite male movie star.
- Renee Zellweger was favourite leading lady.
- Desperate Housewives was named top new drama.
- Joey was best new comedy.
- Moore said it was "an historic occasion" that the 31-year-old awards ceremony would name a documentary its best film.
