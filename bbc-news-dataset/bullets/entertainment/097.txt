- Robert De Niro's film Hide and Seek reached the top of the North American box office.
- De Niro previously held the top spot for three weeks with comedy Meet The Fockers.
- Clint Eastwood's Million Dollar Baby, an Oscar hopeful, came in at number three during its first weekend of wide release.
- Last week's top film, Ice Cube's Are We There Yet?, dropped to second place.
- Oscar best-picture nominee Sideways entered the top ten for the first time in its 15th week of release.
- The Aviator was at number six for the weekend.
