- Ocean's Twelve has gone straight to number one in the US box office chart.
- The sequel narrowly beat its 2001 predecessor, Ocean's Eleven which took $38.1m on its opening weekend and $184m in total.
- Ocean's Eleven was directed by Oscar-winning director Steven Soderbergh.
- Ocean's Twelve box office triumph marks the fourth-biggest opening for a December release in the US, after the three films in the Lord of the Rings trilogy.
- Soderbergh returns to direct the hit sequel which reunites Clooney, Pitt and Roberts with Matt Damon, Andy Garcia and Elliott Gould.
- A milder review in the New York Times dubbed the sequel unabashedly trivial.
