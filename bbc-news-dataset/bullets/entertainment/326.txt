- Number of British films produced in the UK fell 40% in 2004
- UK Film Council said drop was partly due to 2003 being an especially good year for British film production
- Government closed tax loopholes that many film investors had taken advantage of to fund films in UK
- Due to announce a replacement for UK film tax relief scheme Section 48 in July
- Strength of the pound against the dollar is expected to have resulted in a fall in number of US films shot in UK during 2004
