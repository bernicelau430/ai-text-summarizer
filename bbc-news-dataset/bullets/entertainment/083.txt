- Vera Drake received seven nominations at this year's British Independent Film Awards.
- Dead Man's Shoes received eight nominations.
- My Summer of Love and Enduring Love are also nominated.
- Contenders for best film are Vera Drake, Dead Man's Shoes, My Summer of Love, Touching the Void, and Shaun of the Dead.
- Shane Meadows and Kevin McDonald received best director nominations.
- Paddy Considine is nominated for best actor for Dead Man's Shoes.
- Considine is also nominated for best supporting actor award for My Summer of Love.
