- Patti Smith has been made artistic director of this year's Meltdown festival.
- The festival will be held at London's South Bank Centre in June.
- Last year's festival was directed by Morrissey and included appearances by Jane Birkin, Nancy Sinatra, and Alan Bennett.
- Patti Smith says it's important to explore the new and salute the best art aesthetically and spiritually.
- Morrissey persuaded The New York Dolls to reform for a reunion show at last year's festival.
