- Conductor Marcello Viotti has died in Germany at 50.
- Viotti was director of Venice's famous La Fenice Theatre.
- Viotti's time at La Fenice coincided with its reopening in 2003 after it was destroyed by fire in 1996.
- Viotti, director of La Fenice since 2002, conducted at renowned opera houses worldwide.
- Viotti's debut at the New York's Metropolitan Opera came in 2000.
- Viotti was widely acclaimed for his production of the French composer Massenet's Thais.
