- Producers of the Lord of the Rings musical have abandoned plans to premiere the show in London due to the unavailability of a suitable theatre.
- The world premiere will take place in Toronto in March 2006.
- The musical is not expected in London before December 2006.
- The music is written by Bollywood composer AR Rahman and the Finnish group Varttina.
- The production is described as a hybrid of text, physical theatre, music, and spectacle.
