- A Very Long Engagement received 12 nominations for France's Cesar film awards.
- A court judged the film was too American to compete in French film festivals.
- The Cesar organizers modified their rules to allow the film to compete.
- The film received best actress, picture, and director nominations.
- Other films in the best film category include Police drama 36, Quai Des Orfevres, Arnaud Desplechin's Kings And Queen, Abdellatif Kechiche's L'Esquive and France's number one film at the 2004 box-office The Chorus.
