- Germaine Greer has criticised Celebrity Big Brother's "bullying" of housemates after quitting the reality TV show.
- She also said that her fellow housemates had publicity-seeking "agendas".
- Greer said Big Brother had behaved "like a child rather than a parent" by taunting contestant John McCririck after denying him a cola drink.
- Greer had earlier failed to persuade other contestants to stage a naked protest against Big Brother.
