- Comedy Meet The Fockers has held on to the number one spot at the North American box office for a second week.
- It took $19.1m (£9.9m) on Christmas Day alone, the highest takings on that day in box office history.
- It took $42.8m (£23.7m) at the weekend, making its overall takings more than $163m (£90m) in 12 days, according to studio estimates.
- It also broke the box office records for the most money taken on New Year's Eve, when it made $12.2m (£6.2m), and New Year's Day, when it took $18m (£9.4m).
