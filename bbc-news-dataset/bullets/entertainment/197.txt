- John Peel's BBC Radio 1 show will be replaced by three hosts.
- Rob Da Bank, Ras Kwame, and Huw Stephens will fill the slot.
- Radio 1 said it was not trying to replace Peel, but keep his legacy alive.
- Huw Stephens has been one half of the Thursday night show Bethan and Huw in Wales, which explores new music, especially up-and-coming acts in Wales.
