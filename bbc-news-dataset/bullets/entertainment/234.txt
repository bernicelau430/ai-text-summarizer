- Elvis Presley has scored his 19th number one single in the UK charts with Jailhouse Rock.
- Elvis' number one is the 999th in chart history.
- In at number three was Iron Maiden for the Number Of The Beast.
- In the album charts, the Scissor Sisters went to number one with their self-titled release.
- SonyBMG is releasing Elvis's 18 number one singles at the rate of one a week in Britain.
