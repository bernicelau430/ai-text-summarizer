- Ocean's Twelve, a sequel starring George Clooney, Brad Pitt, and Julia Roberts, premiered at number one in the US box office.
- The sequel narrowly surpassed its 2001 predecessor, Ocean's Eleven, which took $38.1m (£19.8m) on its opening weekend and $184m (£95.8m) in total.
- Ocean's Twelve marks the fourth-biggest opening for a December release in the US, following the Lord of the Rings trilogy.
- Steven Soderbergh directs the sequel, reuniting Clooney, Pitt, Roberts, Matt Damon, Andy Garcia, and Elliott Gould.
