- Meet The Fockers held on to the number one spot at the North American box office for a second week.
- It broke box office records for money taken on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.
- It took $19.1m (£9.9m) on Christmas Day alone.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% on 2003's figures.
- The success could help produce record box office revenue for 2004.
- It took $42.8m (£23.7m) at the weekend.
- Overall takings more than $163m (£90m) in 12 days.
