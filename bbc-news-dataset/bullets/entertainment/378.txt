- Debra Hill worked on Halloween with John Carpenter, and he called working with her 
- one of the greatest experiences of my life.
- 
- Hill also worked with Carpenter on Escape From New York and The Fog.
- She was a pioneering woman in film, taking on jobs commonly held by men in the 70s.
- In the 90s, she worked in TV but reunited with Carpenter for Escape From LA in 1996.
- At the time of her death, she was working on a film about the last two men pulled from the rubble of the Twin Towers.
- Halloween, directed by John Carpenter, made over $60m worldwide.
