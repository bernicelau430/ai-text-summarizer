- Armand Marie Leroi's book about the evolution of mutants won the Guardian First Book Award 2004.
- The award is open to books from genres including fiction, poetry, biography, memoir, history, politics, science, and current affairs.
- The judges included Hari Kunzru, Ali Smith, Richard Eyre, and Alexei Sayle.
- Ms. Armistead praised the book's scope, elegance, and inquisitiveness.
- Richard Eyre called Marie Leroi's work "extraordinarily thought provoking".
