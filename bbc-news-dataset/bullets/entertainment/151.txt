- The Sound of Music stage production will be performed in the Austrian capital for the first time, 40 years after the film's release.
- The film was never shown in Austrian cinemas and wasn't broadcast on television until the early 1990s.
- Sensitivities about Nazism and issues with the von Trapp family may have contributed to Austria's reluctance.
- The musical is based on the true story of the von Trapp family, who escaped Nazi-occupied Austria in 1938.
- The song "Edelweiss" was an invention by Rodgers and Hammerstein.
