- U2 wants to be the biggest band ever.
- The band has done "everything in their considerable powers" to remain the biggest band in the world, according to Q magazine editor Paul Rees.
- Bono: "The challenge is to be bigger and bolder and better - to make records the whole world will listen to."
- They've sold 125 million albums around the world.
- Vertigo, the first single from the new album, went straight into the UK singles chart at number one.
- Songs like Sunday Bloody Sunday and New Year's Day brought success and an image as a political and spiritual band - which Bono rejected as a cliche.
