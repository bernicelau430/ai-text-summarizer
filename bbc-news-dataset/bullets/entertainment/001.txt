- A Christmas tree at London's Tate Britain art gallery can receive text messages.
- Sculptor Richard Wentworth will decorate the tree with broken plates and light bulbs.
- The spruce has an antenna to receive Bluetooth texts from visitors.
- It is the 17th year that the gallery has invited an artist to dress their Christmas tree.
- Wentworth's reputation grew in the 1980s, and he has been an influential teacher.
