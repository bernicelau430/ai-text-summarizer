- The term "urban" is debated and seen as a new name for all styles of black music.
- Some believe the term is a politically correct way to describe music that originated from a black background.
- Others argue that the term is used to undermine the status of good music into a commodity.
- The term 'urban' developed from the controversy surrounding the branding of the MOBO Awards.
- Joss Stone's win has reignited the debate about what urban music is.
- Some people think that the term should include Hip-Hop, Soul & Funk but not rock or pop
- Some suggest ignoring the labels and enjoying the music for what it is.
