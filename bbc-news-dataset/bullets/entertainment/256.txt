- Mira Nair directed the film adaptation of William Makepeace Thackeray's novel Vanity Fair.
- Nair cast Reese Witherspoon as Becky Sharp, and the film has a new ending where Becky Sharp runs off for a new life in India.
- Nair said that she was thrilled to direct the film as the book has been a favorite throughout her life.
- The film includes two song-and-dance routines in a Bollywood style.
- Nair defended her decision to cast Witherspoon, despite the rest of the cast being British or Irish.
- Nair said that Thackeray's experience of being born in India and moving to England gave him a unique perspective on his society.
