- Robbie Williams' Angels was named the best song of the past 25 years by BBC Radio 2 listeners.
- Many people disagree with this selection
- Some think Angels is not even the best song in Robbie's repertoire.
- Some think the vote was influenced by Robbie's good looks rather than the song itself.
- Others believe there are many more deserving songs from the last 25 years.
- McFly won the best pop act prize.
- Gwen Stefani picked up the best international female artist award.
- Keane won best British album and breakthrough act.
- Franz Ferdinand won best rock act and best British group.
- Will Young won the best single prize for Your Game.
- Little Britain comedy duo Matt Lucas and David Walliams presented the best song prize to Robbie Williams.
