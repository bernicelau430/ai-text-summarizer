- Rapper 50 Cent and his protege The Game have ended their public feud, aiming to be good role models.
- 50 Cent donated $150,000 to The Boys Choir of Harlem, while The Game gave $103,500.
- 50 Cent launched the G-Unity Foundation to help people overcome obstacles.
- The truce occurred on the anniversary of the death of the Notorious BIG.
- 50 Cent has been known for feuds, including one with rapper Ja Rule.
