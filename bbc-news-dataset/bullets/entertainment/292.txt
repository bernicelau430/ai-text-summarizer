- Julia Roberts will play the part of a spider in a new film version of children's classic Charlotte's Web.
- The film - a mix of live action and animation - will be Roberts' first project since the birth of her twins.
- An animated version was made in 1973 but this will be the first live action film.
- Oprah Winfrey will voice a goose, John Cleese will voice a sheep and Steve Buscemi a rat in the 2006 film.
