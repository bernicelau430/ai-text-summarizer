- Preview performances of the Billy Elliot musical have been delayed to give the child actors a less arduous rehearsal schedule.
- The director, Stephen Daldry, cited the need to protect the young stars and allow them to familiarize themselves with the production.
- Child labor laws limit the amount of time the young actors can work each week.
- Adult actors in the show include Tim Healy and Haydn Gwnne.
- Daldry directed the successful film version of Billy Elliot.
