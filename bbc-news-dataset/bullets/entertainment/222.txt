- U2 aims to remain the biggest band in the world and wants to continue to evolve.
- In 1987, The Joshua Tree broke sales records, making U2 a major rock act.
- Their third album, War, was a breakthrough in the UK and US.
- U2 has explored different branches of rock and electronic dance music, with extravagant stage shows, while trying to retain their mass appeal.
- Vertigo, the first single from How To Dismantle An Atomic Bomb, reached number one in the UK singles chart.
- Before the release of How To Dismantle An Atomic Bomb, U2 had sold 125 million albums worldwide.
