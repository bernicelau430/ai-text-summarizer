- Five writers won their respective categories on January 6th for the Whitbread Prize.
- Andrea Levy is the favorite to win the main Whitbread Prize for Small Island.
- The book has already won the Orange Prize for fiction.
- Susan Fletcher is a contender for Eve Green, which won the first novel prize.
- John Guy's biography of Mary Queen of Scots is also in the running.
- Michael Symmons Roberts' Corpus is another contender.
- Fletcher is a graduate of the University of East Anglia's creative writing course.
- Guy has published many histories, including one of Tudor England.
