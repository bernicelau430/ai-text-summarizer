- Sideways won best film and best actor (Paul Giamatti) from the Chicago Film Critics Association (CFCA).
- Alexander Payne was named best director and won best screenplay shared with Jim Taylor.
- Thomas Haden Church won best supporting actor, and Virginia Madsen won best supporting actress for Sideways.
- The Southeastern Film Critics also awarded Sideways its best film of the year accolade.
- Sideways has been nominated for a Golden Globe.
