- John Peel's BBC Radio 1 show will be succeeded by three shows hosted by three DJs.
- The new shows will focus on diverse, non-commercial music.
- The DJs will continue to host their current shows on Radio 1.
- The show will not try to replace Peel, but will keep his legacy alive with unpredictable music.
- Huw Stephens is one half of the Radio 1 Thursday night show Bethan and Huw in Wales.
- One Music is not new to Radio 1 as it already exists as a website.
