- Fergus Crane won two Smarties Book Prizes.
- It won the category for six- to eight-year-olds.
- It won the 4Children Special Award chosen by after-school club members.
- The story is about a boy who is taken on an adventure by a flying horse.
- The prize is celebrating its 20th year and is billed as "the UK's biggest children's book award".
- The judges chose the winners from an exceptionally strong and varied shortlist.
