- Julia Roberts will play a spider in a new film version of Charlotte's Web.
- The film combines live action and animation.
- This is Roberts' first project since the birth of her twins.
- An animated version was made in 1973, but this will be the first live-action film.
- Oprah Winfrey will voice a goose, John Cleese will voice a sheep, and Steve Buscemi a rat.
