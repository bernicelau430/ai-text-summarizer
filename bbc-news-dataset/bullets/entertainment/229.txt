- New York electro-rock group The Bravery topped the BBC News website's Sound of 2005 poll.
- Bloc Party came in second, and UK rapper Kano came in third.
- The Bravery has been compared to The Cure and New Order.
- Artists in the poll must not have had a UK top 20 single, been on a TV talent show, or already be famous.
- The Bravery was described as "spiky but in love with pop".
