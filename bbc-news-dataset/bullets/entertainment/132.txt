- Comedy Meet The Fockers has held on to the number one spot at the North American box office for a second week.
- Meet the Fockers took $42.8m (£23.7m) at the weekend, making its overall takings more than $163m (£90m) in 12 days.
- The success of Meet the Fockers could help produce record box office revenue for 2004.
- Meet the Fockers broke the record for the most money taken on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.
- Christmas takings were down 26.5% on 2003's figures.
