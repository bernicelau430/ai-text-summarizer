- Actress Patsy Rowlands, known for her roles in the Carry On films, has died at the age of 71.
- Rowlands starred in nine of the popular Carry On films.
- She made her first Carry On film in 1969 where she appeared in Carry On Again Doctor.
- She also appeared in Carry On at your Convenience, Carry On Matron and Carry On Loving, as well as others.
- She won a scholarship to the Guildhall School of Speech and Drama scholarship when she was just 15.
