- Audrey Tautou will play the female lead in the film adaptation of The Da Vinci Code.
- The movie version of Dan Brown's best-selling novel is being directed by Ron Howard and also stars Tom Hanks.
- Ron Howard chose Tautou for the part, preferring a French actress to a big name Hollywood star.
- Jeunet was responsible for directing Tautou in Amelie in 2001, which launched the actress into the mainstream.
