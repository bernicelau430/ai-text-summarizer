- Julia Roberts will play a spider in a new film version of Charlotte's Web.
- This will be the first live-action film version of the children's book.
- An animated version of Charlotte's Web was made in 1973.
- Roberts' participation in the film marks her first project since the birth of her twins.
- Oprah Winfrey, John Cleese, and Steve Buscemi will voice other animal characters in the film.
