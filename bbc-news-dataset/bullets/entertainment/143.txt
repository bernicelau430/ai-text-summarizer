- Scissor Sisters won a gig of the year award for their performance at this year's V Festival.
- The award was voted for by listeners of Virgin Radio.
- The top 10 was mostly dominated by newcomers on the music scene.
- Franz Ferdinand earned a place at number nine for their home-town performance in April.
- Oxfam's Make Fair Trade gig was at number seven, featuring REM, Razorlight and Chris Martin.
