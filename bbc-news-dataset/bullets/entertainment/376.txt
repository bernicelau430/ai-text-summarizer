- Mira Nair was thrilled to make William Makepeace Thackeray's novel Vanity Fair.
- Nair said that her film had picked up on the way Thackeray had seen the world.
- Nair also defended her decision to cast an American actress - Reese Witherspoon - as Becky Sharp.
- The film has a new ending, with Becky Sharp running off for a new life in India.
- Nair has made the film in a Bollywood style, including two song-and-dance routines in the film's second half.
