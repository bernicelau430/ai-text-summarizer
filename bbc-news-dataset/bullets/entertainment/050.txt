- Bruce Willis is suing Revolution Studios over an injury he suffered while making Tears of the Sun.
- The lawsuit said Willis has endured extreme mental, physical and emotional pain and suffering.
- Willis is seeking medical expenses after he said he was hit in the head by a firework during the filming of the 2002 movie.
- The lawsuit said Revolution Studios and special effects foreman Joe Pancake had a duty to Willis to ensure that the squibs were inspected, set up, placed and detonated in a safe manner.
