- 50 Cent became the first solo artist to have three singles in the US top five in the same week.
- The Game's debut reached the top of the charts five weeks prior to this achievement.
- 50 Cent also appears on The Game's song How We Do, which is number four in the US.
- Usher previously scored a chart first with three concurrent singles in the US Top 10, a feat matched by The Bee Gees and The Beatles.
