- Vera Drake has scooped seven nominations at this year's British Independent Film Awards
- Dead Man's Shoes received eight nominations
- Also in the running for awards are My Summer of Love and Enduring Love
- Joining Vera Drake and Dead Man's Shoes in the running for best film are My Summer of Love, Touching the Void and Shaun of the Dead
- Australian star faces competition from Daniel Craig, Phil Davis, Ian Hart, and Paddy Considine
- Considine is also nominated for a best supporting actor award for My Summer of Love
