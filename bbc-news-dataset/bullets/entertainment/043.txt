- Hollywood stars paid tribute to Ossie Davis at a funeral in New York.
- Ossie Davis, a civil rights activist, died in Miami at 87.
- Former US president Bill Clinton and singer Harry Belafonte attended the service.
- Belafonte gave the eulogy, and had been friends with Davis for over 60 years.
- Davis frequently collaborated with director Spike Lee.
- Attallah Shabazz, the daughter of Malcolm X, recalled the eulogy delivered by Davis at her father's funeral.
