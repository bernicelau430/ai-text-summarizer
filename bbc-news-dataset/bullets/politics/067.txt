- Neil Kinnock has been made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992.
- He was recently a Britain's EU commissioner.
- Kinnock will advocate for Upper House reform and talk about higher education.
- He will be known Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- Kinnock has become chairman of the British Council.
- He will be a working peer for practical political reasons.
