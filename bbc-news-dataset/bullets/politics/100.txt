- Mr. Mubanga was allegedly sent to Guantanamo after being interrogated by a British man from MI6 in Zambia in 2002.
- He claims he was brutally interrogated and daubed with urine at the camp.
- Mubanga said he was asked to be an agent but refused and was then sent to Guantanamo Bay.
- Mubanga said, "I've lost three years of my life, because I was a Muslim."
- The Department of Defense maintains Mubanga was properly detained as an enemy combatant.
- He said he was in Afghanistan and Pakistan to study Islam.
- He had dual British and Zambian nationality and was released from Guantanamo in January.
- Menzies Campbell said it was vital to establish whether ministers approved Mubanga's transfer.
