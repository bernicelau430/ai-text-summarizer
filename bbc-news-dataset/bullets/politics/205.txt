- Archbishop Desmond Tutu called for the release of the remaining inmates at Guantanamo Bay and terror suspects detained without trial in the UK.
- Detentions without trial were "unacceptable" and "distressing."
- Tutu criticised the measures, saying: "I am opposed to any arbitrary detention that is happening, even in Britain."
- Foreign nationals detained in the UK are being held at Belmarsh and Woodhill prisons.
- Civil rights group Liberty calls on the government to either free or charge the detained men.
