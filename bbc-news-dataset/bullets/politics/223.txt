- Neil Kinnock was made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992 and was an EU commissioner.
- He will advocate for reform of the House of Lords.
- He will also talk on issues like higher education.
- He will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- He has also become chairman of the British Council.
