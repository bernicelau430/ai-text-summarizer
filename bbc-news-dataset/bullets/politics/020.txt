- Cardinal Cormac Murphy-O'Connor has said billions spent on conflict in Iraq and the Middle East should have been used to reduce poverty.
- Both the Cardinal and Archbishop of Canterbury Dr. Rowan Williams appealed for weapons inspectors to be given more time in Iraq before the war.
- The cardinal will touch on the theme of peace on Friday night at Westminster Cathedral.
- The cardinal said 2005 should be the year for campaigning to make history poverty.
- He questions how a happy Christmas can be wished for those suffering in Iraq, the Holy Land, or Africa unless people say and do what makes for peace.
