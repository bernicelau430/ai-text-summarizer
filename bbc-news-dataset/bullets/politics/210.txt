- Range of services in Wales affected as civil servants joined the UK-wide strike.
- Civil service is a major employer in Wales.
- Thousands of civil servants were on strike across Wales in protest at planned job cuts.
- The protest is about defending jobs and also local services across the country.
- Action follows announcement that 104,000 jobs would be cut, around 6,000 in Wales.
- The worst-affected area in Wales will be the Department of Work and Pensions where 2,000 jobs are threatened.
- The strike was the biggest by civil servants in a decade.
