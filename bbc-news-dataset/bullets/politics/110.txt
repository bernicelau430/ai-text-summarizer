- Neil Kinnock has been made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992.
- He was one of Britain's EU commissioners until recently.
- He will use the Upper House to advocate for reform.
- He will be known Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- He has also become chairman of the British Council.
- He is a working peer for practical political reasons.
