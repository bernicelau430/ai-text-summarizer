- Hundreds of ballot papers for the regional assembly referendum in the North East have "disappeared".
- The Darlington Council spokesman said: "Initially we had complaints from a couple of residents in Mowden to say they thought they should have had their ballot papers by now.
- The Royal Mail is investigating to see if they can find out what the problem is."
- The Royal Mail said: "We are investigating a problem with the delivery route in the Mowden area of Darlington."
- No-one will not receive their ballot paper as special hand deliveries will take place where necessary."
- The commission was satisfied that measures had been put in place to ensure all voters received ballot papers in time.
