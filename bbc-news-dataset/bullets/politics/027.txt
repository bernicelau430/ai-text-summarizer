- Neil Kinnock was made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992.
- He was an EU commissioner until recently.
- He was a critic of the House of Lords, but will advocate for its reform.
- He will talk about higher education in the Upper House.
- He will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- He has also become chairman of the British Council.
- He entered the House of Lords for practical political reasons.
