- Public service workers may strike over pension changes.
- The government plans to raise the pension age from 60 to 65.
- Unions say workers will pay more but get less.
- A co-ordinated one-day strike has been suggested.
- The Trades Union Congress is discussing the issue.
- The Cabinet Office is reviewing the Civil Service Pension Scheme.
- The government says unions will be consulted.
- Public sector pension schemes need to remain affordable and sustainable.
