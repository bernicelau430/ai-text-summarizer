- Neil Kinnock made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992.
- He was an EU commissioner until recently.
- Kinnock will advocate for reform in the House of Lords and talk on issues like higher education.
- He will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- Kinnock also became chairman of the British Council.
