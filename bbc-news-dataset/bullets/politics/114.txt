- Edinburgh City Council is to ballot constituents on proposals to introduce congestion charging in the capital.
- Ballot will ask residents to vote for or against the council's proposed congestion charge scheme and transport improvements.
- Thousands of residents may not be able to vote in Edinburgh's transport referendum.
- Residents who have exercised their right to have their details left off the list could miss the chance to vote.
- Local authorities can only send ballot papers to those on the edited list over non-statutory matters.
