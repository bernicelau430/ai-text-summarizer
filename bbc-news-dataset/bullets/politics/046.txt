- Neil Kinnock was made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983 to 1992 and was an EU commissioner.
- He will advocate for House of Lords reform and higher education.
- He will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- Kinnock has become chairman of the British Council.
- He joined the House of Lords for practical political reasons.
