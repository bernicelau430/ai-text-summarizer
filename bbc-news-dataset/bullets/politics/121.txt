- Welsh Health Minister Jane Hutt: no patient would wait more than 12 months for an outpatient appointment by March 2006.
- Current target: 18 months.
- Total number of people waiting more than 18 months as an inpatient in Wales has fallen by 9.8%.
- Target for an inpatient appointment would remain at 12 months for March 2006.
- 305,775 people on a waiting list of some kind, down from 311,764 last month.
- The number waiting over 12 months also fell from 7,613 to 6,630, or 12.9%.
