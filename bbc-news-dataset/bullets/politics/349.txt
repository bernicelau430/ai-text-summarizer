- Neil Kinnock was made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983 to 1992 and was an EU commissioner.
- He will advocate for reform of the House of Lords and discuss higher education.
- Kinnock will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- He has also become chairman of the British Council.
