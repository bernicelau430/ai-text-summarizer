- Ballot papers for the regional assembly referendum in the North East have disappeared.
- Darlington Council said they sent out the ballot papers, the problem is with Royal Mail.
- Royal Mail is investigating a problem with the delivery route in the Mowden area of Darlington.
- Initially there were complaints from a couple of residents in Mowden who thought they should have had their ballot papers by now.
- The commission was satisfied that measures had been put in place to ensure all voters received ballot papers in time.
- Special hand deliveries will take place where necessary.
