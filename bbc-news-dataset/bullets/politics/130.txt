- Tony Blair is being urged to use all his negotiating powers to end poor countries' debt and double aid.
- Mr Blair has described Africa as a scar on the world's conscience, has already said tackling world poverty would be one his G8 priorities along with climate change and the Aids epidemic.
- Global poverty threatens our shared prosperity and security.
- The world's poorest children are paying for rich countries' policies in aid and debt with their lives.
- New threats to the peace and security of rich nations arise from poverty and gross inequalities.
- For rich countries, providing aid to help to end global poverty is an obligation and a matter of justice, not an act of charity.
