- The Tories have promised a reduction on council tax bills for pensioners.
- Labour wants to retain the property-based tax but reform it.
- The Liberal Democrats want to replace council tax with a local income tax.
- The Tories propose a rebate covering half of the council tax bill for households where council tax payers are over 65, up to £500.
- They would aim for a £5,000 tax-free personal allowance, or a £7,000 allowance for the over-65s.
- The Tories would have the Inland Revenue administer the tax.
