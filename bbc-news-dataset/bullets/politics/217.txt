- Record numbers of schools across the UK are to take part in a mock general election backed by the government.
- The mock votes will take place around 5 May, widely expected to be the date of the general election.
- 600 schools have already signed up for the Y Vote Mock Elections 2005.
- The Y Vote Mock Elections mirror the excitement and buzz of a real election, raising awareness of citizenship, and the benefits of active democracy.
- Young people will develop the enthusiasm to take part in the future.
