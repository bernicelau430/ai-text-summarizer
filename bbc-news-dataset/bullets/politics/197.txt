- Neil Kinnock was made a life peer in the House of Lords.
- He was accompanied by Baroness Amos and Baroness Royall of Blaisdon.
- Kinnock led Labour from 1983-1992.
- He was an EU commissioner until recently.
- He will advocate for reform of the House of Lords and speak on issues like higher education.
- Kinnock will be known as Baron Kinnock of Bedwellty.
- He has become chairman of the British Council.
- He accepted the peerage to be a working peer for political reasons.
