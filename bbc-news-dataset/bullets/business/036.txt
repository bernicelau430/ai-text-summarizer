- Euronext is poised to launch a bid for the London Stock Exchange
- The LSE rejected a takeover proposal from German rival Deutsche Boerse
- Euronext already has a presence in London due to its 2001 acquisition of London-based options and futures exchange Liffe
- Trades on the LSE are cleared via Clearnet, in which Euronext has a quarter stake
- Euronext, which also operates an exchange in Lisbon, last week appointed UBS and ABN Amro as additional advisors
