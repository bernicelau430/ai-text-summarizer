- UK's jobless total rose for the second month in a row in December.
- The number of people out of work rose by 32,000 to 1.41 million in the last three months of 2004.
- Despite this, the number of people in work increased by 296,000 throughout 2004 to 28.52 million.
- Manufacturing sector continued to decline, with 104,000 workers being cut.
