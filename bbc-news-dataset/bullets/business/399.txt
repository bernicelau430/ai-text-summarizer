- India's Oil & Natural Gas Corp (ONGC) is in talks to buy the former assets of troubled Russian crude producer Yukos.
- ONGC may buy 15% of Yukos' former key oil production unit for $2bn.
- India produces about 793,000 barrels of oil per day.
- India imports about 70% of its total oil consumption.
- India's economy is booming and the country's thirst for oil is strong.
- Rosneft now owns Yugansk and Russia has said it will turn the oil producer into a stand-alone firm.
- Yukos has been battling the Russian authorities for more than a year and has filed for bankruptcy protection in the US.
