- US retail sales fell 0.3% in January, the biggest monthly decline since last August.
  - Driven down by a heavy fall in car sales.
- Excluding the car sector:
  - US retail sales were up 0.6% in January
  - Sales rose by just 0.3%.
- In December, overall retail sales rose by 1.1%.
- US retail spending is expected to rise in 2005, but not as quickly as in 2004.
