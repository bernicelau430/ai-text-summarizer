- GM is recalling nearly 200,000 vehicles in the US on safety grounds.
- Affected vehicles are from the 2004 and 2005 model years.
- A pressure accumulator in the braking system could crack during normal driving.
- Fragments could injure people if the hood was open.
- GM expects earnings this year to be lower than in 2004.
- GM is calling back 17,815 Buick Raniers, Chevrolet Trailblazers, GMC Envoys and Isuzu Ascenders from the 2005 model years.
- The windshield is not properly fitted and could fall out in a crash.
