- Deutsche Boerse and the London Stock Exchange (LSE) are to meet amid takeover bid talks.
- The LSE rejected Deutsche Boerse's proposed £1.3bn offer in December.
- Euronext is due to hold talks with LSE and is reportedly ready to raise £1.4bn to fund a bid.
- LSE shareholders feared a Deutsche Boerse takeover would force them to use Clearstream.
- Euronext, owner of Liffe in London, is also interested in bidding for LSE.
