- China overtook the US to become Japan's biggest trading partner in 2004
- China accounted for 20.1% of Japan's trade, compared with 18.6% for the US
- Japan's trade surplus grew 17.9% to 12.011 trillion yen, with more than half the surplus accounted for by its trade with the US
- This is the highest figure for Japanese trade with China since records began in 1947
