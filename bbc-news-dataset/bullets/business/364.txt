- UK condom maker SSL International may be subject to a takeover early in 2005.
- SSL International has refused to comment on the reports.
- GPW is starting due diligence work on SSL International for a corporate client.
- Reckitt Benckiser is a high-profile firm that might woo SSL.
- Analysts have seen SSL as a takeover target for years.
- SSL was formed by a three-way merger between Seton Healthcare, footwear specialists Scholl and condom-maker London International Group.
