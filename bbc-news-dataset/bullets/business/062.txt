- EBay forecasts 2005 revenue of $4.2bn to $4.35bn and earnings of $1.48 to $1.52 per share (excluding items).
- Analysts had estimated 2005 revenues of $4.37bn and earnings of $1.62 per share (excluding items).
- For 2004, eBay earned $778.2m on sales of $3.27bn.
- Excluding special items, eBay's profit was 33 cents a share, but analysts had expected 34 cents.
