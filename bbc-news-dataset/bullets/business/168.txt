- BMW announced £100m of new investment in the Mini, less than four years after its launch.
- The target of 100,000 Mini models a year was repeatedly raised due to high demand.
- BMW had invested some £280m in Mini production before Wednesday's announcement.
- Mini Convertible was launched last spring, with a waiting list for the top model.
