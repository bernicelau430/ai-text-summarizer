- Krispy Kreme Doughnuts' shares fell after announcing it would restate its 2004 financial reports.
- Accounting errors caused the restatement, cutting profits by $3.8m to $4.9m.
- This follows a previous warning that earnings would be cut by up to 7.6% due to accounting errors.
- Some franchise owners are not in compliance with their loan agreements.
- Krispy Kreme may need to borrow extra money to cover franchisee debts or leases.
- Shares sank 14.87% to close at $10.48.
