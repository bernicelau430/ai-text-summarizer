- Fiat was set to restructure its business after reaching a $2bn settlement with GM.
- Steps being considered include listing Ferrari and bringing Maserati and Alfa Romeo closer together.
- The Financial Times said Fiat may transfer Maserati within its wholly- owned Alfa Romeo division.
- Maserati and Ferrari sell about 10,000 cars between them and both companies broke even in 2003.
- Fiat owns a 56% stake in Ferrari.
