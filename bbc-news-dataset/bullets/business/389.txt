- The Egyptian government is planning to privatise one of the country's big public banks.
- Bank of Alexandria will be sold sometime in 2005.
- It has not been decided whether the sale will be a public flotation.
- Bank of Alexandria or Banque du Caire was expected to be sold first.
- The big four state banks are Banque Misr, National Bank of Egypt, Banque du Caire, and Bank of Alexandria.
