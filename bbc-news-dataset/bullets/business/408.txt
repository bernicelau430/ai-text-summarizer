- Turkey's investment in Iran's mobile industry may be scrapped.
- Iran's parliament voted to cut Turkcell's stake in a new mobile network from 70% to 49%.
- Turkcell said it is prepared to accept a minority stake if Iran will award it the mobile deal.
- Observers believe Turkcell is set to pull out of the $3bn deal.
- Turkcell's mobile deal is the second Turkish investment in Iran to encounter difficulties.
