- German business confidence fell in February knocking hopes of a speedy recovery in Europe's largest economy.
- Ifo president Hans-Werner Sinn said any improvement in German domestic demand was sluggish.
- Economy and Labour Minister Wolfgang Clement called the dip in February's Ifo confidence figure a very mild decline.
- Ifo figures and Germany's continuing problems may delay an interest rate rise by the European Central Bank.
- Ifo confidence index fell to 95.5 in February from 97.5 in January, its first decline in three months.
