- Physical damage in Sri Lanka estimated at $1.3 to $1.5bn.
- President Kumaratunga called for a peaceful solution to internal conflict for reconstruction efforts.
- ILO estimates 400,000 Sri Lankans lost their jobs, mostly in tourism, fishing, and agriculture.
