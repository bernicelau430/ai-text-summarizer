- The economy has grown for six quarters but growth slowed dramatically in the last quarter amid weaker global demand.
- Japanese industrial output fell in October while unemployment rose.
- The decline was led by a fall in demand for electronic parts for mobile phones and digital televisions.
- Although inventories fell 0.7% month on month, they were 36% higher than a year ago.
- Japanese government officials played down the latest data, arguing that domestic consumer demand was still resilient.
