- Gross mortgage lending stood at £17.9bn, compared with £21.8bn in January last year.
- Lending for house purchases and gross mortgage lending were 29% and 18% lower year-on-year respectively.
- Overall, mortgage lending rose by £4bn in January, a smaller increase than the £5.1bn seen in December.
- Both the Council of Mortgage Lenders (CML) and Building Society Association (BSA) said lending was down sharply.
