- Jobless total rose for the second month in a row in December.
- Number of people out of work rose 32,000 to 1.41 million in the last three months of 2004.
- Number of people in work increased by 296,000 to 28.52 million in 2004.
  - Highest figure since records began in 1971.
- Manufacturing sector continued to suffer, with 104,000 workers axed during the last quarter of 2004.
  - Pushing employment in the sector to a record low of 3.24 million by the end of last year.
