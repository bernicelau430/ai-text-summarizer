- US industrial production continued to rise in November.
- Output from factories, mines and utilities rose 0.3% from a revised 0.6% increase in October.
- Mining output drove the increase, surging 2.1%, while factory output rose 0.3%.
- The US economy grew at an annual rate of 3.7% between July and September.
- Jobs growth averaged 178,000 during the same period.
- The latest increase means industrial output has grown 4.2% in the past year.
