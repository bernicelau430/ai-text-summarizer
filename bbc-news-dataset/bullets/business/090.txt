- The French government will provide 70m euros ($91m) in aid to its struggling wine industry.
- The support aims to make French producers more competitive.
- The French wine industry faces declining consumption at home and popularity of New World wines.
- Efforts to promote French wine abroad will be boosted by 3.5m euros.
- 15m euros in low interest loans will be available to wine cooperatives.
- The government will seek EU approval to distil 250 million litres of excess wine into alcohol.
