- French economy is growing but without an improvement in employment.
- High oil prices will weigh on consumer spending and confidence.
- Finance minister warns that high oil prices posed a threat to French growth.
- The French statistics agency expects the economy to grow by about 2.4% this year.
- Growth is buoyed by strong consumer spending and business investment.
- French companies are keen to protect their profit margins at a time of rising energy costs.
- Companies are reluctant to take on extra staff.
