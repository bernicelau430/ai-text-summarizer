- The plan is likely to give creditors of Parmalat Finanziaria shares worth about 5.7% of the debts they are owed.
- Creditors of Parmalat are likely to see the percentage of debt they receive fall from 7.3% to 6.9%.
- Creditors' debts are expected to be converted into shares through two new share issues amounting to more than 2bn euros.
- Parmalat hopes to be back on the Italian stock exchange in July.
