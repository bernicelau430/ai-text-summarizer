- Brazil's unemployment rate fell to its lowest level in three years in December, reaching 9.6%.
- The jobless rate average for the whole of 2004 was 11.5%, down from 12.3% in 2003.
- Average monthly salaries grew 1.9% in December 2004 from December 2003.
- Average monthly wages fell 1.8% in December to 895.4 reais from November.
