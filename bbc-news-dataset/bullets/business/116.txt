- Euronext is poised to launch a bid for the London Stock Exchange (LSE).
- LSE rejected a takeover proposal from Deutsche Boerse.
- Euronext already has a presence in London through its acquisition of Liffe.
- Trades on the LSE are cleared via Clearnet, in which Euronext has a quarter stake.
- Euronext has appointed UBS and ABN Amro as additional advisors.
- Concerns raised about potential job losses in Germany if a merged company is run out of London.
