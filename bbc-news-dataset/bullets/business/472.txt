- China has ordered a halt to construction work on 26 big power stations, including two at the Three Gorges Dam.
- The State Environmental Protection Agency said the projects had failed to do proper environmental assessments.
- The biggest single project halted was the Xiluodi Dam project.
- The 26 projects had failed to do proper environmental assessments.
- The damming of the Upper Yangtze has also begun to attract criticism from environmentalists in China.
