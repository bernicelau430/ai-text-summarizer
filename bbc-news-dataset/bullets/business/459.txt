- Deloitte warns that 10 years of golden economic performance may end in 2005.
- Growth is likely to slow markedly in 2005 and 2006 compared to last year's 3.25% expansion.
- Deloitte forecasts economic growth of 2% this year, below the Chancellor's forecast of 3% to 3.5%.
- Economic growth will be constrained by increased pressure on household budgets and rising taxes.
- The housing market slowdown will negatively impact household spending growth.
