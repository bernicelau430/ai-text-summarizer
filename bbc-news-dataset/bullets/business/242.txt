- Citigroup has sold its Travelers Life & Annuity insurance arm to Metlife for $11.5bn
- The company will pay between $1bn and $3bn in Metlife stock with the rest being made up of cash
- For Metlife, the purchase gives it access to a much larger distribution network
- Travelers had sales of $5.2bn in 2004 and made a profit of $901m
- It has total net assets of $96bn
