- The US-registered Albanian Macedonian Bulgarian Oil Corporation (AMBO) will build the pipeline.
- Albania, Bulgaria, and Macedonia have approved the construction of a $1.2bn oil pipeline.
- The pipeline aims to provide alternative ports for shipping Russian and Caspian oil.
- The 912km pipeline will run from Burgas (Bulgaria) to Vlore (Albania), crossing Macedonia.
- The pipeline will cross from the Black Sea to the Adriatic coast.
- Albanian Prime Minister Fatos Nano calls it important for regional, EU, and Euro-Atlantic integration.
