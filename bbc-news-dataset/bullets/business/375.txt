- LG Card was threatened with collapse due to huge debts.
- Creditors and its former parent rescued the firm.
- South Korea's largest credit card firm averted liquidation with a one trillion won bail-out.
- Creditors own 99% of LG Card.
- LG Card returned to profit in September but needed further capital.
- LG Card has seven million customers.
- Collapse of LG Card would have sent shockwaves through the country's economy.
- LG Card will not need any more financial aid after this.
