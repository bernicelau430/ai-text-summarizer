- Ten years of "golden" economic performance may come to an end in 2005 with growth slowing markedly, City consultancy Deloitte has warned.
- Deloitte is forecasting economic growth of 2% this year, below Chancellor Gordon Brown's forecast of 3% to 3.5%.
- Economic growth is likely to be constrained during the next few years by increased pressure on household budgets and rising taxes, Deloitte believes.
