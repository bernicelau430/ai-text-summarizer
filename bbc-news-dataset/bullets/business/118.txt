- In 2004, Ericsson had a net profit of 19bn kronor, a significant improvement from the 10.8bn kronor loss in 2003.
- The operating profit for the three months ending December 31 was 9.5bn kronor, up from 6.3bn kronor the previous year.
- Fourth-quarter sales increased by 9% to 39.4bn kronor.
- Total sales for the year rose to 131.9 billion kronor from 117.7bn kronor in 2003.
- Ericsson shares decreased by 5.9% to 20.7 kronor in early trading on Thursday.
