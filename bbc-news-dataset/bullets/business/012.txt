- The UK's jobless total rose for the second month in a row in December, official figures show.
- The number of people out of work rose 32,000 to 1.41 million in the last three months of 2004.
- Throughout 2004, the number of people in work increased by 296,000 to 28.52 million - the highest figure since records began in 1971.
- 104,000 manufacturing workers were axed during the last quarter of 2004.
