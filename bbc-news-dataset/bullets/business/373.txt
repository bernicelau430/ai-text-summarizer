- The Boix-Vives family, owners of Rossignol, are considering an offer from Quiksilver.
- The Boix-Vives family has been looking for possible buyers for Rossignol for the past couple of years.
- Rossignol also manufactures golf equipment, snowboards, and sports clothing.
- Analysts believe other sporting goods companies may now consider purchasing Rossignol.
- This could prompt an auction and increase the sale price.
- Shares of Skis Rossignol have jumped as much as 15% due to speculation of a purchase by Quiksilver.
