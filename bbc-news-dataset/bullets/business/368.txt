- The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is in deficit of £23bn (£12m).
- The Financial Economists Roundtable (FER) is requesting that Congress act.
- There is an estimated $2.9bn hole in its pilots' pension scheme.
- The PBGC will guarantee the scheme.
- The FER says firms should not have been allowed to reduce insurance premiums paid to the PBGC fund.
- Professor Dennis Logue suggests Congress avoid taxpayers picking up the bill.
- The FER wants Congress to change the PBGC's funding rules.
