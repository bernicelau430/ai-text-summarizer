- Hyundai Motor plans to build a second plant in India to meet the country's growing demand for cars.
- The new plant would produce 150,000 cars a year.
- South Korea's top car maker estimates that the Indian market will grow 15% this year, to 920,000 vehicles, reaching 1.6 million vehicles by 2010.
- Hyundai expects its sales in India to grow 16% to 250,000 in 2005.
