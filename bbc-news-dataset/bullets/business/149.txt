- GM will build a new Cadillac BLS at its Saab factory in Sweden.
- The car will compete in the medium-sized luxury car market.
- It will not be sold in the US.
- Bringing Cadillac production to Sweden should help introduce scale to the Saab factory.
- GM aims to push Cadillac as an alternative luxury brand for European drivers.
- Production shift could reduce exposure to the weak US dollar.
- Saab's losses could be reduced by shifting some production to the US.
