- ISM manufacturing index's employment index eased to 52.7 in December from 57.6 in November.
- ISM's "prices paid" index eased to 72.0 from 74.0.
- ISM's "new orders" index rose to 67.4 from 61.5.
- ISM said growth was driven by a "significant" rise in new orders.
- ISM's index of national manufacturing activity rose to 58.6 last month from 57.8 in November.
- The chairman of the ISM's survey committee said that this completes a strong year for manufacturing based on the ISM data.
