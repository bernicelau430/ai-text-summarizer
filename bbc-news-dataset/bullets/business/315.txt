- Scottish and Newcastle (S&N) is to buy 37.5% of India's United Breweries.
- S&N will buy a 17.5% equity stake in United and make a public offer to buy another 20% stake.
- In 2002 S&N and United agreed to form a strategic partnership.
- S&N is to invest 2.47bn rupees in United through non-convertible redeemable preference shares.
- United's budget airline, Kingfisher Airlines, is to buy 10 A320 aircraft from Airbus.
