- Standard & Poor's has upgraded Russian bonds to "investment grade" from "speculative".
- The agency cites improved government finances due to soaring oil sales and a budget surplus.
- S&P said the solidity of government finances outweighed the political risk.
- Soaring oil sales and a budget surplus mean Russian debt is no longer a risky investment.
- Other ratings agencies like Fitch and Moody's have already upgraded Russia's sovereign debt.
