- Citigroup sold its Travelers Life & Annuity insurance arm to Metlife for $11.5bn.
- The company will pay between $1bn and $3bn in Metlife stock, with the rest in cash.
- Travelers had sales of $5.2bn in 2004 and made a profit of $901m.
- For Metlife, the purchase gives it access to a much larger distribution network.
- Travelers Life & Annuity has total net assets of $96bn.
