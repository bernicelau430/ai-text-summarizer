- London's Savoy hotel has been sold to a group combining Saudi billionaire Prince Alwaleed bin Talal and a unit of HBOS bank.
- Prince Alwaleed also has stakes in Fairmont Hotels & Resorts and Four Seasons.
- Fairmont plans to invest $48m in renovating parts of the Savoy.
- This includes the River Room and suites with views over the River Thames.
