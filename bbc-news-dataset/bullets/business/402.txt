- The Fed has implemented quarter percentage point rises in June, August, September, and November.
- The Fed aims to combat price growth and lift interest rates from their 40-year lows in a "measured" way.
- Analysts warn the Fed to be careful not to move too aggressively and take the wind out of the recovery's sails.
- The Fed feels it has a fair amount of flexibility to pause or tighten more quickly depending on economic growth and inflation.
