- Richard Scrushy is accused of "directing" a $2.7bn accounting fraud at HealthSouth.
- HealthSouth was co-founded by Scrushy in Alabama in 1984.
- Several former HealthSouth employees have already pleaded guilty to fraud.
- Former HealthSouth employees are expected to give evidence against Mr Scrushy.
- Defence lawyers said Mr Scrushy had been deceived by other executives.
- Prosecutor Alice Martin said they will present evidence that Richard Scrushy knew about and participated in the conspiracy and profited from it.
