- Parmalat Finanziaria plans to re-list shares on the Italian stock exchange in July.
- Creditors will receive shares worth about 5.7% of the debts they are owed.
- Creditors of Parmalat are likely to see the percentage of debt they receive fall from 7.3% to 6.9%.
- Debts will be converted into shares through two new share issues of over 2bn euros.
- Parmalat went bust after an accounting scandal.
