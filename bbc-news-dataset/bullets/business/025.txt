- Court ruling is a blow to efforts to get damages for the sale of Yugansk.
- Yukos argued for US court jurisdiction due to local bank accounts and CFO in Houston.
- Judge dismissed Yukos' attempt to gain bankruptcy protection in the US.
- Yukos claimed Russian courts and the European Court of Human Rights were either unfriendly or offered less protection.
- The vast majority of Yukos' business and financial activities continue to occur in Russia.
- US court's jurisdiction had been challenged by Deutsche Bank and Gazpromneft.
- Russia had indicated it would not abide by the rulings of the US courts.
