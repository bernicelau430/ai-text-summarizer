- Turkey's investment in Iran's mobile industry looks set to be scrapped.
- Iran's parliament voted to cut Turkcell's stake in a mobile network from 70% to 49%.
- Turkcell has said it is prepared to accept a minority stake.
- Turkcell's mobile deal is the second Turkish investment in Iran to run into trouble.
- Observers believe Turkcell will pull out of the $3bn deal.
