- Turkcell's mobile deal is the second Turkish investment in Iran to run into trouble
- Turkey's investment in Iran's mobile industry looks set to be scrapped after its investment was slashed
- Iran's parliament voted to cut Turkcell's stake in a new mobile network from 70% to 49%
- Turkcell is prepared to accept a minority stake if Iran will award it the mobile deal
- Observers believe Turkcell is set to pull out of the $3bn deal
- The company will continue to monitor developments
