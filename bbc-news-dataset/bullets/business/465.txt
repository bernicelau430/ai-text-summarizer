- The trial of Bernie Ebbers, former chief executive of WorldCom, has started in New York.
- Mr. Ebbers is accused of masterminding an $11bn accounting fraud.
- The fraud led to the firm's collapse in July 2002.
- Under Ebbers, WorldCom became a $160bn telecoms giant.
- The trial is expected to last two months.
- Ebbers could face a substantial jail sentence if found guilty.
