- Trial of Bernie Ebbers, former CEO of WorldCom, has started in New York.
- Mr. Ebbers is accused of being the mastermind behind an $11bn accounting fraud.
- Under Mr. Ebbers' leadership, WorldCom became a $160bn telecoms giant.
- Mr. Ebbers' trial is expected to last two months.
- If found guilty, Mr. Ebbers could face a substantial jail sentence.
