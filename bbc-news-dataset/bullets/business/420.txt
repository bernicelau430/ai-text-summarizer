- India has signed a $40bn deal to import millions of tonnes of liquefied natural gas from Iran.
- Firms led by the Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) will also assist in the development of Iranian oil fields.
- ONGC and the National Iranian Oil Company (NIOC) reached a preliminary deal for Indian firms to take part in the development of the Yadavaran and Jufeyr oilfields.
