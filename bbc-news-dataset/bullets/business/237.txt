- Over 800,000 Britannia Building Society members will receive a profit share.
- The average profit share is worth £52 per member.
- Last year, members shared £42 million with an average payment of £38.
- Britannia no longer makes payments less than £5.
- Members qualify for profit share with a mortgage or investment account (excluding deposit accounts).
- Customers must have been members for at least two years by December 31, 2004, to qualify for this year's payment.
