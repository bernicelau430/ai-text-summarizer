- More than 800,000 Britannia Building Society members are to receive a profit share worth on average £52 each
- To qualify for the profit share, members must have either a mortgage, or an investment account other than a deposit account
- To qualify for this year's payment, customers must have been members for at least two years on 31 December 2004
- Britannia has also stopped making payments to members if they are worth less than £5
