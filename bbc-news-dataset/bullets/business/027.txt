- Mittal Steel could cut up to 45,000 jobs over the next five years.
- Mittal Steel has operations in 14 countries.
- Once the acquisition of International Steel Group is completed, the company will aim to reduce its workforce by between 7,000 and 8,000 annually.
- No decisions on job cuts have been made yet.
- Mittal Steel was formed last year when LNM Holdings merged with Dutch firm Ispat.
- Lakshmi Mittal has told investors the combined company will have to shed thousands of jobs.
