- Federated Department Stores is set to acquire May Department Stores for $11 billion.
- Federated will also assume $6 billion of May's debt, bringing the total deal value to $17 billion.
- Federated's annual sales are $15.6 billion, while May's are $14.4 billion.
- The merger is expected to boost Federated's earnings from 2007.
- Federated anticipates $1 billion in one-off charges related to the deal.
- May has faced challenges competing with larger groups like Federated and retailers like Wal-Mart.
- The acquisition will combine stores like Macy's, Bloomingdale's, and Marshall Field's.
- This merger will create the largest department store chain in the US.
