- Parmalat Finanziaria detailed administrators' latest plans for re-listing the shares of the group
- Parmalat hopes to be back on the Italian stock exchange in July
- Creditors' debts are expected to be converted into shares through two new share issues
- Creditors of Parmalat, the main operating company, are likely to see the percentage of debt they receive fall from 7.3% to 6.9%
- The plan is likely to give creditors of Parmalat Finanziaria shares worth about 5.7% of the debts they are owed
