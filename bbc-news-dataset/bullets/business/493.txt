- China's economy expanded by 9.5% in 2004, faster than predicted.
- Investment in fixed assets was up 21.3% from the previous year.
- Growth in industrial output was 11.5% in 2004, down from 17% the previous year.
- China raised rates by 0.27 percentage points to 5.8% in October 2004.
- Officials insist they are not ready to shift to a more market-set rate.
