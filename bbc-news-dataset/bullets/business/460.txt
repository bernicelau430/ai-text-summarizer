- Oracle is cutting about 5,000 jobs following the completion of its $10.3bn takeover of Peoplesoft last week.
- Oracle will retain more than 90% of Peoplesoft product development and product support staff.
- Oracle's chief executive said it would deliver on the development and support commitments.
- A Peoplesoft sign had been turned into shrine to the company, with flowers, candles and company memorabilia.
- Oracle began notifying staff of redundancies on Friday and the process would continue over the next 10 days.
