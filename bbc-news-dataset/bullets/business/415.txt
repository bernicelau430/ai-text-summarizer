- UK house prices dipped slightly in November, the Office of the Deputy Prime Minister (ODPM) said.
- Annual house price inflation is still strong, up 13.8% in the year to November.
- The average house price fell marginally to £180,226, from £180,444 in October.
- All areas saw a rise in annual house price inflation in November except for Northern Ireland and the West Midlands, where the rate was unchanged.
- In London, annual inflation rose only slightly in November to 7.1% from 7% the previous month.
