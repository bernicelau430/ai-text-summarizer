- Qantas is considering moving up to 7,000 jobs overseas to cut costs.
- This move could affect cabin and maintenance staff.
- Unions have criticized the potential move, arguing that Qantas is profitable.
- Qantas confirmed it is exploring recruiting and sourcing products overseas, possibly through joint ventures.
- Qantas CEO Geoff Dixon stated the airline needs the lowest cost structure possible, necessitating increased overseas sourcing.
- Qantas claims it will continue to create jobs in Australia despite the potential offshore shift.
