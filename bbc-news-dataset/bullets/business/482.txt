- The WEF report shows that concern among businesses regarding HIV/Aids has dropped by 23% in the last 12 months.
- Across sub-Saharan Africa, only around 7% of companies have formal HIV/Aids policies in place.
- Over 65% of business leaders surveyed could not estimate the prevalence of HIV among their staff.
- The WEF report concludes that businesses need to understand their exposure to HIV/Aids risks and develop good local practices to manage them.
