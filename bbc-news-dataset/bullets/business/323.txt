- Deloitte warns that ten years of "golden" economic performance may end in 2005 with growth slowing.
- Deloitte is forecasting economic growth of 2% this year, below Chancellor Gordon Brown's forecast.
- Economic growth is likely to be constrained by increased pressure on household budgets and rising taxes.
- Reduced household spending will result in a marked slowdown in growth in 2005 and 2006.
