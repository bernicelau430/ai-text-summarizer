- American Express is to spin off its less profitable financial advisory subsidiary.
- This would boost its profitability.
- The unit was acquired by American Express 20 years ago as Investors Diversified Service, of Minneapolis.
- AEFA has more than 12,000 advisers selling financial advice, funds and insurance to 2.5 million customers.
- Shares in American Express surged more than 8% on Tuesday after the announcement.
