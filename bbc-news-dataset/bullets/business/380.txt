- India will allow domestic commercial airlines to fly long haul international routes to increase competition and reduce prices.
- Jet Airways is India's premier private domestic carrier.
- Air Sahara is ranked third in the same category.
- India is trying to grow its airline industry due to booming economic growth increasing demand for travel.
- Jet Airways and Air Sahara will initially benefit from this move.
- Air travel in India has increased by almost 20% from the previous year.
- Further increases in air travel are expected.
