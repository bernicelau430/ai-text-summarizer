- BMW is recalling cars with faulty diesel fuel-injection pumps from Robert Bosch.
- The recall affects pumps made in December and January and does not represent a safety risk.
- BMW will extend a production break at one of its plants.
- Audi faces production bottlenecks due to a shortage of unaffected Bosch parts.
- DaimlerChrysler (Mercedes Benz) will halt some production.
- Audi and Mercedes Benz received the defective pumps but don't need to recall vehicles.
