- Sri Lanka faces a $1.3bn bill in 2005 for reconstruction after the tsunami.
- The estimate is preliminary and could rise in 2006.
- Half the IMF support could come from a freeze on debt repayments.
- The bank has 5bn rupees set aside to lend at a lower interest rate to those who lost property.
- The value of the rupee has risen as foreign funds have flooded into the country.
