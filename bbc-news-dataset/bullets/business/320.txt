- The World Trade Organisation (WTO) is to hold membership talks with both Iraq and Afghanistan.
- Before the process gets under way, all 148 WTO members must give their backing to applicant countries.
- Membership of the WTO helps guarantee a country's goods receives equal treatment in the markets of other member states.
- Iran's bid to join the trade body has been refused after the US blocked its application for the 21st time.
- There are now 27 countries seeking membership of the WTO.
