- For the whole of 2004, GM earned $3.7bn, down from $3.8bn in 2003.
- Annual revenue rose 4.5% to $193bn.
- Net profits fell 37% in the last quarter of 2004.
- GM earned $630m in the October-to-December period, down from $1bn in Q4 2003.
- Profits were hit by higher healthcare costs in the US and losses at its European operations.
