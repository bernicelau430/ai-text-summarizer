- Mrs. Carroll was born in the same year plastic cards first appeared in the UK.
- In 2004, UK shoppers are expected to spend £269bn on plastic cards.
- This is compared to £268bn paid with cash.
- Spending on credit and debit cards has overtaken cash spending in the UK for the first time.
- The first plastic cards appeared in the UK in June 1966, issued by Barclaycard.
- Initially, only a few retailers accepted them and very few customers held them.
