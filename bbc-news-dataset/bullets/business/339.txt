- The BCC reported worrying signs for manufacturing despite positive news for the export sector.
- Confidence increased in both manufacturing and service sectors but failed to reach early 2004 levels.
- The service sector outlook was "uncertain" despite increased exports and orders.
- The UK manufacturing sector faces "serious challenges" over the next two years.
- The UK economy still faces "major risks" and growth is set to slow.
- Reduced threat of interest rate increases improved confidence.
- Exchange rates were cited as a major concern despite the rise in confidence.
