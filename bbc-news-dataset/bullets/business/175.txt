- The National Statistic Institute (INSEE) added that consumer spending in January rose 3.8% on a year-on-year basis.
- INSEE also said that French consumer prices fell 0.6% in January, but were up 1.6% on an annual basis.

- Car sales fell 2.8% in January, following a fall of 0.6% in December.
