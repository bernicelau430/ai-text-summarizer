- The Karachi Stock Exchange (KSE) recorded its largest single day gain, surging 3.5%.
- The KSE index closed at 6709.93, an overall gain of nearly 400 points in the first two trading days of the week.
- Analysts cite solid economic growth, corporate profitability, and improved credit ratings as reasons for the market's performance.
- Some analysts believe Arab money and recent political developments have also contributed to the market's rise.
