- Less than a quarter of companies (24%) get their corporate social responsibility (CSR) reports independently verified.
- The UK government will require all quoted companies to report their social and environmental risks in their annual reports.
- Nearly 20% more UK top 250 firms produced non-financial reports this year than last year.
- 24 UK top 250 companies reported for the first time this year.
- The quality of reports has improved.
- 76% of the 145 companies reporting didn't examine their supply chains.
- The survey is published by Context and SalterBaxter.
