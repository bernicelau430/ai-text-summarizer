- Hearts of Oak set up an all Ghanaian Confederation Cup final with a 3-2 win over Cameroon's Cotonsport Garoua in Accra on Sunday.
- Hearts of Oak started the game needing a win to qualify for the final while Cotonsport only needed to avoid defeat to go through.
- Hearts will play Asante Kotoko in the two-leg final, after the Kumasi team qualified from Group A on Saturday.
