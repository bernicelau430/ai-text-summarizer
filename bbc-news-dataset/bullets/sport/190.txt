- Jimmy Connors is planning a long-term relationship with the Lawn Tennis Association to help unearth the next Tim Henman.
- Connors spent three days at the LTA's annual Elite Performance winter camp in La Manga.
- Connors said he was impressed with the coaches and the way the kids went about their workouts.
- LTA wants to use Jimmy for a number of weeks a year and hope it's the beginning of a long-term relationship.
