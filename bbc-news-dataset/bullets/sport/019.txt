- London Marathon organisers are hoping that Susan Chepkemei will still take part in this year's race on 17 April.
- Chepkemei took part and finished third in last Sunday's world best 10K race in Puerto Rico.
- Chepkemei has finished second to Paula Radcliffe in the 2002 and 2003 London races as well as in November's New York City marathon.
