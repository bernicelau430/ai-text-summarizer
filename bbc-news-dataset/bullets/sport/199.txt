- Arjen Robben has broken two metatarsal bones in his foot and will be out for at least six weeks.
- The injury occurred when he was kicked by Blackburn midfielder Aaron Mokoena.
- Robben claims he knew something was wrong instantly.
- Chelsea physio Mike Banks hopes Robben could return in March.
- This metatarsal injury is different from the traditional one that has kept Scott Parker out for two months.
