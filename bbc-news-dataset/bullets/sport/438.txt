- Andy Robinson was livid after England was denied two tries in their Six Nations loss to Ireland.
- He believes Mark Cueto scored a legal try and the video referee should have been consulted on Josh Lewsey's play.
- Robinson stated that England did everything possible to win and that they were cost two tries.
- South African referee Jonathan Kaplan ruled that Cueto was ahead of Charlie Hodgson when he gathered the fly-half's cross-field kick.
