- Lawrence Dallaglio's career has been marked by both success and controversy.
- He was part of the England side that won the first Rugby Sevens World Cup in 1993.
- He was offered the England captain's band within two years of his debut.
- He made the 1997 Lions tour to South Africa.
- He stepped down as England skipper after allegations of drug use.
- Dallaglio's crowning glory was his part in the side that won the Rugby World Cup in 2003.
- He was the only player to play every minute of England's World Cup triumph in Australia.
- He led by example as one of the few members of a squad lacking many World Cup stars to live up to expectations.
