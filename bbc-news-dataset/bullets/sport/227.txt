- Kenteris and Thanou will fight their two-year bans imposed on them by the IAAF at a tribunal.
- The sprint duo face charges of failing to give information and refusing to submit to a drugs test.
- The panel will also decide on the two-year ban imposed on their coach Christos Tzekos.
- They also missed tests in Tel Aviv, Chicago and Athens before the Games.
- Eight criminal charges have been laid against the sprinters as well as Tzekos.
