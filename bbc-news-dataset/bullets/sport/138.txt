- Graeme Souness has reopened his dispute with Craig Bellamy after claiming the Welshman was "not good enough" for Newcastle.
- Bellamy left Newcastle to join Celtic on loan after a major row with Souness.
- Souness said he risked losing the confidence of the players and damaging his own reputation if he had not taken a hard line after Bellamy accused him of lying.
- Souness said Bellamy did not score enough goals.
