- Sania Mirza won the Hyderabad Open, becoming the first Indian to win a WTA singles title.
- She beat Bondarenko, playing in her first WTA final.
- Mirza started nervously, committing double faults.
- She broke Bondarenko again in the next game to clinch the title.
- Mirza broke serve twice in a row and held on to her advantage to take the first set.
- She re-established the lead and established 5-2 lead in the deciding set.
