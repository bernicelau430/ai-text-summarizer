- The Italians travel to Edinburgh hoping to claim their first away win in the Six Nations.
- Italy have only recorded three wins since they joined the Six Nations in 2000.
- Two of those wins have come against Scotland.
- Italy skipper Marco Bortolami believes Saturday's Six Nations contest against Scotland will be a scrappy encounter.
- Both sides are looking for their first win of the championship.
- The third-round game at Murrayfield has been billed as a wooden-spoon decider.
