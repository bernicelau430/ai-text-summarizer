- Andy Robinson was livid after his side were denied two tries in Sunday's 19-13 Six Nations loss to Ireland in Dublin.
- Robinson believes Mark Cueto scored a perfectly legal try.
- He also thought the referee should have checked the video for Josh Lewsey's try.
- England has lost their first three matches in this year's Six Nations and four out of their six games since Robinson took over from Sir Clive Woodward in September.
- Robinson believes Ireland has every chance of winning this Six Nations.
- Referee Jonathan Kaplan ruled that Cueto was ahead of Charlie Hodgson when the fly-half hoisted his cross-field kick.
