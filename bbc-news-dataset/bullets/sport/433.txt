- Mido apologized to the Egyptian people in an attempt to rejoin the national team.
- Shalaby said that after an apology Mido would be available for the national side if coach Hassan Shehata chose him.
- Mido said he came specially from England to Egypt to rejoin the national team and to apologise for all his mistakes.
- Shalaby welcomed the apology and said no one had exerted pressure on Mido to apologise.
- Mido added that it's not his right to give orders and say when he wants to play.
- Mido added that he will always make sure that he put the national's team's matches as his top priority.
- Shalaby said Mido's apology does not negatively affect Mido in anyway, on the contrary it makes him a bigger star and a role model for all football players.
