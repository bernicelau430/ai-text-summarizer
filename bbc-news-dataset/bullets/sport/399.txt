- Wigan chairman Maurice Lindsay says he doesn't expect a quick solution to Andy Farrell's potential move to rugby union.
- Money is not a motivating factor for the club, despite the lucrative nature of the move.
- Lindsay understands rugby union's interest in Farrell.
- The Rugby Football Union, league, club, and England coaching team have a say in the decision.
- Lindsay says Farrell is a loyal friend to the club and not deserting them.
- Lindsay admitted the news came as a surprise.
