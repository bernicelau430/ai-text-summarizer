- Mia Hamm has played her final game.
- She ends her career as the most prolific scorer in international football with 158 goals in 276 games.
- Hamm, 32, officially retired after this year's Athens Olympics.
- She helped the US claim a 5-0 win over Mexico in her final game.
- Hamm will now embark on a new life with husband, Chicago Cubs shortstop Nomar Garciaparra.
