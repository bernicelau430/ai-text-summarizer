- Irish rugby had a successful year, with the senior squad winning the Triple Crown.
- Ireland defeated World Cup champions England at Twickenham.
- The under-21 team finished runners-up to England in the Six Nations tournament.
- The under-19 team reached the semi-finals of the World Cup.
- Ireland defeated South Africa at Lansdowne Road, their second victory over them after 1965.
- Gordon D'Arcy was named Six Nations Player of the Tournament.
- Tragedies also hit Irish rugby, including the deaths of John McCall and Mike Doyle.
- Ulster finished runners-up in their group for the second season and lost the Celtic League final to the Scarlets.
- Munster again flew the flag for the Irish.
