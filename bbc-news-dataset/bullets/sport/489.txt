- Marat Safin has ruled out any chance of winning Wimbledon in the future.
- Safin won the 2000 US Open against Sampras.
- Safin lost in two Australian Open finals.
- Safin said he had "given up" on Wimbledon after losing in round one last year.
- Safin is hopeful that winning the Australian Open will give him the belief he needs to win more Grand Slam titles.
