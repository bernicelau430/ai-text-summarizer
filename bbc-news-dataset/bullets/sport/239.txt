- Arjen Robben has broken two metatarsal bones in his foot and will be out for at least six weeks.
- Robben knew instantly something was wrong when he was felled by Blackburn midfielder Aaron Mokoena.
- Chelsea physio Mike Banks was hopeful that Robben could return at some point in March.
- Robben had an MRI scan on the injury, sustained during the Premiership win at Blackburn, on Monday.
