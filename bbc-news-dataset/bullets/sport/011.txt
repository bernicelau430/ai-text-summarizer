- Paula Radcliffe has been granted extra time to decide whether to compete in the World Cross-Country Championships.
- Hayley Yelling said the team would understand if Radcliffe opted out of the event.
- Zara Hyde Peters of UK Athletics said Paula would be a huge asset to the GB team.
- Radcliffe was world cross-country champion in 2001 and 2002 but missed last year's event because of injury.
