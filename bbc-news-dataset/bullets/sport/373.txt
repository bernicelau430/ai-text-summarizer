- Jonny Wilkinson has been named as England's new rugby union captain for the three November Tests.
- Wilkinson kicked the winning drop-goal in the final seconds of extra-time in England's 2003 Rugby World Cup triumph against Australia.
- Wilkinson has scored 817 points in 52 internationals.
- Wilkinson played in all three Lions Tests in 2001 and all but one of England's games in the 2003 World Cup.
- Wilkinson said it's the ambition of so many players to one day be captain of England and today I have realised a dream.
