- Greek sprinter Kostas Kenteris has denied claims that he faked a motorbike crash to avoid a doping test.
- Kenteris said that he asked to be tested for banned substances in hospital after the crash.
- Kenteris blamed Greek Olympic Committee officials and his former coach for failing to inform him of the test.
- Kenteris confirmed that he was due to light the flame at the Athens opening ceremony.
- Kenteris and Katerina Thanou are set to learn if they will face criminal charges.
