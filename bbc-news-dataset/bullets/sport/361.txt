- Dave Hodges, Scarlets and USA Eagles forward, ended his playing career to coach in the States.
- Hodges played at Stradey Park for seven years but battled injury for the last two.
- He was Llanelli's player of the season in 2001/2.
- Scarlets chief executive Stuart Gallacher confirmed that 17 of the current squad would be out of contract in the summer.
- Hodges and the club agreed to end his contract early since he would be unable to play this season.
