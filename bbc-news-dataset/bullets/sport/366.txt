- Jonah Lomu aims to play for New Zealand again after overcoming kidney problems.
- Lomu says he is getting stronger every day.
- He has been training three times a day, six days a week.
- Lomu's first 15-man game will be on 4 June.
- He says he has a training schedule which is quite testing and combines with sevens and a whole lot of things.
- Opinion has been divided on whether Lomu should attempt to return to the game after such a major operation.
