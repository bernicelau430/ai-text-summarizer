- Munster's Heineken Cup quarter-final tie against Biarritz has been switched to Real Sociedad's Paseo de Anoeta stadium in San Sebastian
- Ulster were the last Irish team to play at the Paseo de Anoeta stadium
- Real's ground holds 32,000 whereas the Parc des Sports Aguilera in Biarritz has a capacity of just 12,667
