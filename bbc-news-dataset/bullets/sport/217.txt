- Former world number one Jimmy Connors is planning a long-term relationship with the Lawn Tennis Association to help unearth the next Tim Henman.
- Connors spent three days at the LTA's annual Elite Performance winter camp in La Manga earlier this week.
- Connors was impressed with the coaches and the way the kids went about their workouts and the feeling they put into every practice they had.
- The LTA wants to use Jimmy for a number of weeks a year and hopes this is the beginning of a good long-term relationship.
