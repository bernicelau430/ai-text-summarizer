- Ahmed 'Mido' Hossam has apologized to the Egyptian people to rejoin the national team.
- Mido said he came from England to Egypt to rejoin the national team and apologize for his mistakes.
- Egypt FA spokesman Methat Shalaby welcomed the apology and said no one pressured Mido to apologize.
- Mido stated it's not his right to give orders and will prioritize the national team's matches.
- Shalaby believes Mido's apology makes him a bigger star and role model.
