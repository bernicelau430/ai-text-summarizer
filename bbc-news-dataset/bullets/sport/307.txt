- Wales coach Mike Ruddock says England lock Danny Grewcock needs to review his actions after he kicked Dwayne Peel
- The referee's interpretation was that Grewcock was attempting to step over the ruck
- Grewcock said that if the ball is at the back of the ruck and he feels he can step over and disrupt it then he will do that
- Ruddock said that it's up to the citing commissioner
- Grewcock was sin-binned with Wales captain Gareth Thomas for retaliation
- Grewcock and Jason Robinson have both said that the clash with Peel was accidental
- Grewcock was banned from rugby for two months for reckless use of a boot in a match against New Zealand
