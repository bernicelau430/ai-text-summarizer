- Munster's Heineken Cup quarter-final against Biarritz has been switched to San Sebastian.
- Real Sociedad's Paseo de Anoeta stadium will host the game on 3 April.
- Ulster played at the stadium in 1998 during a pre-season tour.
- Real's ground holds 32,000, whereas the Parc des Sports Aguilera in Biarritz has a capacity of just 12,667.
