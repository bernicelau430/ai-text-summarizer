- Andy Roddick ended his 18-month association with coach Brad Gilbert.
- Roddick won 121 of 147 matches while working with Gilbert.
- Roddick said he enjoyed his time with Gilbert.
- Roddick's decision was based on what he thinks is best for his game.
- Gilbert said he has enjoyed all of his time with Andy.
- Roddick hired Gilbert after parting ways with coach Tarik Benhabiles after the 2003 French Open.
