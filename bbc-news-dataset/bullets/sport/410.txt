- Simon Taylor has been named in the Scotland squad for Saturday's Six Nations clash with Italy.
- Both Scotland and Italy have lost their opening two Six Nations games.
- This weekend's game could turn out to be a battle to avoid the wooden spoon.
- Taylor suffered knee ligament damage playing against Ireland in Dublin in the 2004 Six Nations championship.
- Taylor has turned out for Saracens in recent weeks during a short-term loan to improve his fitness.
