- Ian McCall has been given a reprieve from being sacked as Dundee United manager.
- Dundee United players expressed their solidarity with McCall after their Scottish Cup win.
- Chairman Eddie Thompson had a long and detailed talk with McCall.
- The speculation is doing no one any good.
- McCall would have been sacked if Sheffield Wednesday manager Paul Sturrock had been willing to return to Tannadice.
- McCall has only been here five months and doesn't expect to be leaving soon.
- Thompson said they must get out of their current situation through the efforts of current personnel.
