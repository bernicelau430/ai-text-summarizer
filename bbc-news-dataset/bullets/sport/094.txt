- Mido has made another apology to the Egyptian people in an attempt to rejoin the national team.
- Shalaby said that after an apology Mido would be available for the national side if coach Hassan Shehata chose him.
- Mido came specially from England to Egypt to rejoin the national team and to apologize for all my mistakes.
- Shalaby welcomed the apology and said no one had exerted pressure on Mido to apologize.
- Mido said it's not his right to give orders and say when he wants to play but he will always make sure that he puts the national team's matches as his top priority.
- Shalaby said Mido's apology does not negatively affect him in anyway, on the contrary it makes him a bigger star and a role model for all football players.
