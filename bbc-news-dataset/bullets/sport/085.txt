- Derartu Tulu has confirmed she will take part in the BUPA Great Caledonian Run in Edinburgh on 8 May.
- Tulu has won the Boston, London and Tokyo Marathons, as well as the world 10,000m title in 2001.
- Race director Matthew Turnbull said, "We are delighted to have secured the services of one the most decorated competitors the sport has ever seen".
