- Justine Henin-Hardenne lost to Elena Dementieva in a comeback exhibition match in Belgium on Sunday for her second defeat in two days.
- Henin-Hardenne slid down the world rankings in the second half of 2004 after contracting an illness in April.
- She was forced off the circuit for a second time after being knocked out of the French Open in the second round.
- Henin-Hardenne feels positive and says there will be less pressure than last time even if she is champion.
