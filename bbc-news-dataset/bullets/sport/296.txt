- France coach Bernard Laporte believes his team will be scared going into their game with England on Sunday, but claims it will work in their favour.
- Laporte said they will go to Twickenham with a little fear and it'll give them a boost.
- Laporte will announce his starting line-up on Wednesday at the French team's training centre in Marcoussis, near Paris.
- Laporte said playing with the France team has always been a dream and now it has come true and he is about to face England at Twickenham in the Six Nations.
