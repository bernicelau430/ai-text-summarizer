- Jimmy Connors is planning a long-term relationship with the Lawn Tennis Association to help unearth the next Tim Henman.
- Connors spent three days at the LTA's annual Elite Performance winter camp in La Manga earlier this week.
- Connors was impressed with the coaches and the way the kids went about their workouts.
- The LTA wants to use Jimmy for a number of weeks a year.
- LTA performance director David Felgate said that Connors was fantastic with the players and coaches.
