- Liverpool and Millwall have been charged by the FA over crowd trouble during their Carling Cup match
- Liverpool have been charged with one breach for failing to prevent their fans conducting themselves in threatening and/or violent and/or provocative behaviour
- They have been charged with failing to ensure that fans refrained from racist and/or abusive behaviour and for failing to prevent spectators throwing missiles onto the pitch
- Millwall have also been charged over alleged racist behaviour by their supporters
- CCTV footage showed the catalyst for the trouble was a Liverpool fan attacking a Millwall fan
