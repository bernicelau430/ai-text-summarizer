- The Apple Powerbook 100 was chosen as the greatest gadget of all time by Mobile PC magazine.
- The Apple iPod is at number 12, and the first Sony transistor radio is at number 13.
- The Sony Walkman is at number three, and the 1956 Zenith remote control is at number two.
- The Tivo personal video recorder is among the top 10.
- The list also includes the first flash mp3 player, digital camera, and mobile phone.
- The abacus is the oldest "gadget" on the list, dated at 190 A.D.
- The magazine specified that gadgets needed to be self-contained and mobile.
- Other pre-electronic gadgets include the sextant, marine chronometer, and Kodak Brownie camera.
