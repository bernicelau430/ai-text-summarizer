- Voting is under way for the annual Bloggies which recognise the best web blogs - online spaces where people publish their thoughts - of the year.
- The big Bloggies battle will be for the ultimate prize of blog of the year.
- Three new categories have been added to the list this year, including best food, best entertainment, and best writing of a weblog.
- A blog is created every 5.8 seconds, although less than 40% of the total are updated at least once every two months.
- The South-East Asia Earthquake and Tsunami Blog is also nominated in the best overall category.
