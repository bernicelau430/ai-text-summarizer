- Apple unveils new, low-cost Macintosh computer, the Mac mini.
- $499 Macintosh described as the "most important Mac" made by Apple.
- Mr. Jobs also unveiled the iPod shuffle, a new music player using cheaper flash memory.
- The smaller iPod will hold about 120 songs.
- In January, Apple sued a website for publishing specifications for the new computer.
- The machine would appeal to PC-owning consumers who had purchased an iPod.
- iPod already has a 65% market share of all digital music players.
- Will appeal to people thinking of changing operating systems.
