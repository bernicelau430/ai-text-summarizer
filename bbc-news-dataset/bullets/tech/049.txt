- A fake Paypal.com domain has been registered using the Internationalised Domain Name (IDN) system.
- The fake domain looks identical to the real site but could lead users to a fake site for stealing information.
- The fake domain has been handed back to Paypal.
- The UK Internet Forum (UKIF) warns that the IDN system could make it easier for scammers to create fake sites.
- The Internationalised Domain Names system has been approved by the Internet Electronic Task Force.
