- A proposed European law on software patents will not be re-drafted by the European Commission (EC)
- Supporters say current laws are inefficient
- Current laws would serve to even up a playing field without bringing EU laws in line with the US
- Some major tech firms say it is needed to protect inventions
- Others fear it will hurt smaller tech firms
- The EC says the Council of Ministers will adopt a draft version but said it would review "all aspects of the directive"
