- 600,000 connections take the total number of people in the UK signing up for broadband from BT to almost 3.3 million.
- People signing up to broadband get their service direct from BT or via the many companies that re-sell BT lines under their own name.
- More people signed up for broadband in the last three months than in any other quarter.
- The percentage of people using BT lines for voice calls has shrunk to 55.4%.
- Nationally more than 5 million browse the net via broadband.
