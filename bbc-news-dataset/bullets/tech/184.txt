- Peter Molyneux was granted an honor for services to the computer games industry.
- Molyneux has been a pivotal figure in the industry for almost 20 years.
- He was surprised because computer gaming was once thought to be a fad.
- Molyneux left Bullfrog in 1997 to set up Lionhead Studios.
- Lionhead Studios created the game Black & White.
- Molyneux has been behind ground-breaking games like Populous, Theme Park, and Dungeon Keeper.
