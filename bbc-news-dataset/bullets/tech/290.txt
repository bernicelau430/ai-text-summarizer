- New strains of the Cabir mobile phone virus use short-range radio technology.
- New Cabir strains will spread via Bluetooth technology to any vulnerable phone in range.
- F-Secure issued a warning about the new strains of Cabir.
- The viruses do not do any damage to a phone.
- The Cabir virus only affects high-end handsets running the Symbian Series 60 phone operating system.
- There are so far no reports of any phones being infected by the new variants of Cabir.
- A mistake in the original Cabir meant that the bug would only have been able to infect one phone at a time.
