- A fake website (decuk.org) is attempting to exploit the tsunami disaster for fraudulent donations.
- The Disaster Emergency Committee (DEC) is aware of the site and has contacted Scotland Yard's Computer Crime Unit.
- DEC has contacted US net registrars and the hosting firm to shut down the site.
- DEC wants any money donated via the site to be handed over to the official organization.
- None of the email addresses on the site work, and the real owner of the domain is obscured.
- BT and DEC's hosting company are also working to get the site shut down.
