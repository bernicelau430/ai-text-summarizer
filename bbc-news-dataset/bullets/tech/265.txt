- 53 million people in the UK own a mobile phone.
- Half of those phones use GPRS.
- GPRS enables web browsing, news, mobile music, and chat.
- Phones with GPRS and MMS doubled since last year.
- Mobile companies want users to use multimedia functions for increased revenue.
- MDA predicts 75% of UK mobiles will access the net via GPRS by end of 2005.
- Critics find MMS confusing and some mobiles difficult to use.
- Companies are still battling to persuade people to go online and use MMS.
