- Group test of 100 home photo printers for PCs.
- Compared costs to online developers and High Street stores.
- Some printer ink costs more than £2 per millilitre.
- Home printing can still be cheaper than High Street stores.
- High Street photo shops can be cheapest for 6x4in prints.
- 7x5in and 8x10in prints cheaper to produce at home.
- Home prints offer better convenience and quality.
- New printers produce brighter colors and are less likely to fade.
- Producing an 8x10in print on an Epson R800 costs £1.87.
- Worth printing at home if you choose the right printer.
