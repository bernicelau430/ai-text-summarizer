- A US woman is suing Hewlett Packard (HP), alleging that its printer ink cartridges are secretly programmed to expire on a certain date.
- The lawsuit seeks to represent anyone in the US who has purchased an HP inkjet printer since February 2001.
- The cost of printer cartridges has been a contentious issue in Europe.
- A Computeractive Magazine study revealed that the running costs of inkjet printers can be high due to cartridge prices.
- HP ink cartridges use chip technology to sense when they are low on ink.
