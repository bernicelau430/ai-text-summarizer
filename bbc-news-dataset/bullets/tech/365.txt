- Telekom Austria's Engerwitzdorf scheme lets people watch programs on their PC via Aon.
- Villagers upload videos to a Buntes Fernsehen portal for browsing and downloading.
- Aon streams live channels and offers pay-for-download content.
- Roll-out to other areas is slow due to setup work, government backing, and user education.
- Telekom Austria is considering other similar schemes in rural areas due to the project's success.
- A larger TV-on-demand project is launching in Vienna in October.
- Broadband is needed for broadcast quality programs on home PCs.
