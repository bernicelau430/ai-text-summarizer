- Music download networks are proving popular with an audience of youngsters keen to take advantage of free music and with advertisers equally keen to reach out to a captive audience.
- eDonkey is enjoying the benefits of having some very well-known faces advert on its network.
- Current advertisers on the most popular peer to peer service eDonkey who now steadfastly support copyright theft with real cash money include Nat West, Vodafone, O2, First Direct, NTL, and Renault.
- The BPI strongly believes that any reputable company should look carefully at the support they are giving these networks through their advertising revenue.
- Wippit believes it is time advertisers stopped providing 'oxygen' for companies that support illegal downloading.
