- Argonaut Games was put up for sale to save it from running out of cash.
- Argonaut was behind the Harry Potter games, bringing in a healthy flow of cash.
- Staff were told job losses were necessary to save the company.
- Delays in new deals led to Argonaut warning it was running out of cash.
- Argonaut has been making games for about 18 years and is one the largest independent games developers in the UK.
