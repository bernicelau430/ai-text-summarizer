- A system to create website addresses using alphabets like Cyrillic could be exploited by scammers
- A fake Paypal.com has already been registered with net domain giant Verisign
- The resulting domain looks identical to the real site
- The fake domain has been handed back to Paypal
- ASCII codes are used to represent European languages, while Unicode is used for other languages
- The UK Internet Forum (UKIF) is concerned about the system
- The Internationalized Domain Names (IDN) system has been approved by the Internet Electronic Task Force
