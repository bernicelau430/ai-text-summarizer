- Panlogic predicts China's internet users will exceed the US by 2008
- Net use in China has a different character than in many Western nations
- Government restrictions on TV advertising make the internet a source of commercial messages
- Credit cards are rare in China due to debt fears
- Many use the internet instead of phones or to arrange meetups at net cafes
- China's 100 million net users represent less than 8% of the population
