- UWB was previously used as a sensing technology.
- The government is seeking help on regulating Ultra Wideband (UWB) technology.
- Ofcom is seeking opinions on whether to allow UWB on a licence-exempt basis.
- UWB can stream large amounts of data over short distances.
- It can transmit hundreds of megabits of data per second.
- UWB can create Personal Area Networks for gadgets to swap data.
- Use of UWB is currently only allowed in the UK under a strict licencing scheme.
