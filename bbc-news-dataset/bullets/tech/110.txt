- Podcasting is an internet-based radio show.
- Listeners can subscribe to podcasts and have them collected automatically using XML code and RSS.
- Adam Curry, a former MTV host, is a proponent of podcasting.
- Curry records, hosts, edits, and produces a daily podcast called The Daily Source Code.
- Curry wants to make podcasting "the Next Big Thing".
- Podcasting is creating an explosion of content, with podcasters in various countries.
