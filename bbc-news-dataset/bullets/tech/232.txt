- BT reports more people signed up for broadband in the last three months than in any other quarter
- BT adds 600,000 connections, totaling almost 3.3 million broadband subscribers
- Includes those subscribing directly through BT or companies reselling BT lines
- The UK total of 5.3 million translates into 7.5 connections per 100 people
- BT Retail provided about 30% of the new broadband customers
- Surge due to BT extending ADSL reach beyond 6km
- Over 5 million nationally browse the net via broadband
- Percentage of people using BT lines for voice calls has shrunk to 55.4%
