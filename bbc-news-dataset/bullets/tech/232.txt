- Search engines are key to how people use the internet.
- Search engines match keywords to web pages, but many people still type natural language questions.
- The BBC's online search engine was used a record amount in 2004.
- Companies like Google dictate how people travel through websites online.
- The most requested search terms of 2004 included games, EastEnders, CBBC, John Peel, Harry Potter and Olympics.
- Strange enquiries included: what is a dog?, how do you say basketball?, what is the name of James Martin's dog?
