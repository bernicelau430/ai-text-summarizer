- Tata Teleservices uses lasers to connect customers' offices to their core network.
- Laser links are faster to set up than traditional cable connections.
- The company has set up networks in over 700 locations in 12 months using lasers.
- Lightpointe laser bridges work over distances up to 4km.
- The lasers route voice and data at speeds of 1-2mbps, though they are capable of 1.25gbps.
- Getting permission to dig and lay cables is difficult in some areas.
