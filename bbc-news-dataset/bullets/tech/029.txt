- Linus Torvalds led an attack on software patents at a Linux summit in California.
- He stated that software patents are a problem for the open source movement.
- There are between 150,000 and 300,000 registered software patents in the US.
- Open source developers argue that many software patents should not have been granted.
- Intellect spokeswoman said open source and patents have co-existed for many years without problems.
- There are concerns that Microsoft would issue a series of patent lawsuits in the future.
