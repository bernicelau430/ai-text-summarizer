- Ask Jeeves acquired Bloglines to enhance its web indexing capabilities, not just to enter blog publishing.
- Bloglines' database of 300 million articles will be used to improve Ask Jeeves' web index.
- Ask Jeeves will help organize Bloglines' database using its own indexing technology.
- The acquisition will integrate well with Ask Jeeves' My Jeeves service, allowing personalized web experiences.
- Ask Jeeves is focused on helping people find information rather than helping them write it.
- The amount Ask Jeeves paid for Bloglines was not revealed.
