- ScanSoft and Gracenote are developing technology for voice control access to film and music libraries.
- Music and film fans will be able to control their digital media players by speaking to them.
- Pairing voice technologies with Gracenote's music database will bring benefits to consumer devices.
- The firms gave no indication about whether the iPod, or any other media player, were in mind for the use of the voice control technology.
- Portable players are becoming popular in cars, and auto firms are working with Apple to device interfaces to control the iPod.
- They want to give people hands-free access to digital music and films in the car, at home, or on the move.
- Users will also be able to request music that fits a mood or occasion, or a film by saying the actor's name.
