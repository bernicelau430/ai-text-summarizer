- Peter Molyneux was granted an honor for services to the computer games industry
- He is the head of Surrey's Lionhead Studios
- He was surprised to receive the honor, as many people thought computer gaming was a fad
- He left Bullfrog in 1997 to set up Lionhead Studios
- Molyneux has been behind ground-breaking games such as Populous, Theme Park, Dungeon Keeper, and Black and White
- He started in the games industry almost by accident
