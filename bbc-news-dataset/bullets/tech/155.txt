- Blu-Ray and HD-DVD are competing to be the format of choice for future films and games.
- Blu-Ray Association said that games giants Electronic Arts and Vivendi would both support its DVD format.
- Next generation DVDs will hold high-definition video and sound.
- A Blu-ray disc will be able to store 50GB of high-quality data, while Toshiba's HD-DVD will hold 30GB.
