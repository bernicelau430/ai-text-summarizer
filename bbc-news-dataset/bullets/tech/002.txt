- The growing criminal use of malware has meant the end of the neat categorisation of different sorts of viruses and malicious programs.
- With almost any phishing or spyware attack, criminals are guaranteed to catch some people out and have the contacts to exploit what they recover.
- Programs are now crafted for directly criminal ends and firms are tightening up networks with defenses to combat the new wave of malicious code.
- Many of the criminal programs came from Eastern Europe where cash-rich organised gangs can find a ready supply of technical experts that will crank out code to order.
