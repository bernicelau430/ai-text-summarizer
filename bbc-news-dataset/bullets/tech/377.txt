- A new law to protect computer users from spyware is being introduced on January 1.
- California's Consumer Protection Against Spyware Act bans software that takes control of another computer.
- Adware, a form of spyware, collects information on a user's web-surfing.
- Spyware can collect a wide range of information from computers.
- Makers of spying programs could face hefty fines.
- Spyware is a major nuisance and security threat to PC users.
- Users are turning to free programs like Spybot and Ad-Aware for protection.
