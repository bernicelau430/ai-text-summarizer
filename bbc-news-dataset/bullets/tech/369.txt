- The first convictions for piracy over peer-to-peer networks have been handed down in the US
- Two men pleaded guilty to conspiracy to commit felony copyright infringement
- The two men operated central hubs in a piracy community organized across the Direct Connect peer-to-peer network
- The piracy group, the Underground Network, required users to share between one and 100 gigabytes of files
- Raids were conducted in August on suspected pirates by the FBI
