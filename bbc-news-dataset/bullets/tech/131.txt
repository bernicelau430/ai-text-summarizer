- ScanSoft and Gracenote are developing technology for voice control access to film and music libraries
- They aim to give people hands-free access in the car, at home, or on the move
- Technology uses voice to request music based on mood, occasion, or to select a film using actor's name
- Firms gave no indication about whether iPod or any other media player were in mind
- Portable players becoming popular in cars; some auto firms working with Apple for iPod control interfaces
