- A new technology combines digital people with real views for tourists at historical sites.
- A camera captures the view and software combines it with animated virtual elements.
- The goal is to bring the past to life.
- This would allow visitors a glimpse of life as it was originally lived in places such as Pompeii.
- The Lifeplus project combines digital and real views, unlike virtual reality.
- Crucial software interprets the visitor's view and provides an accurate match between real and virtual elements.
