- The first convictions for piracy over peer-to-peer networks have been handed down in the US
- Two men pleaded guilty to one count of conspiracy to commit felony copyright infringement
- The Department of Justice said the two men operated the central hubs in a piracy community organized across the Direct Connect peer-to-peer network
- The piracy group called itself the Underground Network
- Five raids were on the homes of suspected copyright thieves and one on a net service firm
