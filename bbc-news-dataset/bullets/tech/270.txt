- DDoS attacks involve sending large amounts of data to overwhelm sites.
- Many gambling sites are targeted.
- Attacks with gigabytes of data are rare.
- Most attacks hit sites with 10 megabytes of data over short periods.
- Bigger attacks sending down 200 megabytes of traffic or more were rarely seen.
- Intelligent net-based systems are needed to combat DDoS attacks.
- Attacks are often preceded by ransom emails.
- Attacks use thousands of computers in different countries.
- Net firms are collaborating to share information about attacks.
