- Half-Life 2 won six Bafta Awards, including best game and best online game.
- Singstar won the originality award.
- Donkey Konga won the children's award.
- Burnout 3 won three awards in the categories for racing, technical direction and best PlayStation 2 game.
- Halo 2 won the best Xbox game category, while Prince of Persia: Warrior Within was adjudged the best GameCube title.
- Call of Duty: Finest Hour won the audio award and Hitman: Contracts won the music award.
