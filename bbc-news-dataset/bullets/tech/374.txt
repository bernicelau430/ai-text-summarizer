- Commodore was a pioneer in home computing.
- The Commodore 64, launched in 1982, was an affordable home PC.
- Commodore International filed for bankruptcy in 1994 and was sold to Tulip Computers.
- The Commodore computer brand may be resurrected after being bought by a US digital music distributor.
- The Commodore 64 sold more than any other single computer system.
- In the late 1980s, Commodore rivaled Atari.
