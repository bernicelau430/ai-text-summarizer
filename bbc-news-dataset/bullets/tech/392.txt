- ScanSoft and Gracenote are developing technology for voice control of media libraries.
- Music and film fans will be able to control digital media players by speaking.
- Voice technologies combined with Gracenote's music database will bring the benefits of speech to consumer devices.
- The firms gave no indication about whether the iPod, or any other media player, were in mind for the use of the voice control technology.
- Users will be able to request music based on mood or a film by actor's name.
