- The FBI is warning that a computer virus is being spread via e-mails that purport to be from the FBI.
- The attachment in the e-mail contains the virus.
- The FBI does not engage in the practice of sending unsolicited e-mails to the public in this manner.
- The attachment contains a virus that infects the recipient's computer.
