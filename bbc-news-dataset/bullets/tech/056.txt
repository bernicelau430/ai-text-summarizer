- The net helps Chinese people keep in touch with friends.
- Net use in China has a different character than in Western nations.
- Credit cards are rare in China due to fears about debt.
- Government restrictions on TV advertising mean the net is a source of commercial messages.
- Many use the net instead of phones or meet friends at net cafes.
- Analysts predict China's net users will exceed the 137 million US users by 2008.
- China has 100 million net users representing less than 8% of the population.
