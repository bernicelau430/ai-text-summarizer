- The 600,000 connections take the total number of people in the UK signing up for broadband from BT to almost 3.3 million.
- The numbers include those that get their service direct from BT or companies that re-sell BT lines.
- More people signed up for broadband in the last three months than in any other quarter.
- There are now more than 300 different firms offering CPS services.
- The percentage of people using BT lines for voice calls has shrunk to 55.4%.
- The UK has 7.5 connections per 100 people, compared to 6.7 in Germany and 15.8 in the Netherlands.
- BT Retail provided about 30% of the new broadband customers.
- The surge was partly due to BT stretching the reach of ADSL beyond 6km.
- More than 5 million browse the net via broadband.
