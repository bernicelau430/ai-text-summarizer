- Mr. Molyneux, head of Surrey's Lionhead Studios, was granted an honor for services to the computer games industry
- He has been a pivotal figure in the industry for almost 20 years
- Molyneux involvement with the games industry started almost by accident as back in the early days game making was more a hobby than a career
- He was surprised as much because, not too long ago, many people thought computer gaming was a fad
- He left Bullfrog in 1997 to set up Lionhead Studios which was behind the ambitous and widely acclaimed game Black & White
- Molyneux has been behind many of the ground-breaking games of the last 15 years
