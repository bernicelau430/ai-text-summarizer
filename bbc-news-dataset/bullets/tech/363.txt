- A US shop expects the PlayStation Portable (PSP) to sell out on its first day.
- The PSP is considered more of a portable entertainment device than just a gaming device.
- Sony plans to have a million units ready for the US launch.
- The first million units will include the Spider-Man 2 film on UMD.
- The European launch was delayed to ensure sufficient devices for the US and Japanese markets.
- Sony aims to gain a share of the handheld gaming market.
- Nintendo dominates the handheld market with over 90% share in the US.
- Gamers waited over 36 hours to purchase the PSP.
