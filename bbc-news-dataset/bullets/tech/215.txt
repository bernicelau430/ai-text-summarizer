- Following the launch of an ambitious European project, how people receive their digital entertainment in the future could change.
- The Commission wants people to be able to locate the content they desire and have it delivered seamlessly, when on the move, at home or at work, no matter who supplies the devices, network, content, or content protection scheme.
- The European Commission is being pragmatic in its approach.
- The most difficult and challenging area for the Commission will be to identify a solution for different Digital Rights Management (DRM) schemes.
- Such a large and powerful organisation has stated its desire for digital formats to be open to all and work on any gadget.
