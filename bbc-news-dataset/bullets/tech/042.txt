- Skype offers free PC-based calls to other Skype users.
- Users need a free Skype account to make calls via wi-fi without paying for access.
- Skype Out, its paid service, has attracted 940,000 users.
- Broadreach and Skype are rolling out a service at 350 UK hotspots.
- Forthcoming launches include video conferencing, voice mail, and Skype In (receiving calls from landlines and mobiles).
