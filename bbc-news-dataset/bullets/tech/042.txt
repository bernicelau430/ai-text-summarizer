- Proposed European law on software patents will not be re-drafted by the European Commission.
- Supporters say current laws are inefficient and it would serve to even up a playing field.
- Major tech firms say it is needed to protect inventions.
- Others fear it will hurt smaller tech firms.
- The EC says the Council of Ministers will adopt a draft version but review all aspects.
- EU legislation needs approval of both Parliament and the Council of Ministers.
