- BBC Sport is launching a new analysis tool called Piero at a Wales v England rugby match.
- Piero is a 3D analysis tool created by BBC Research and Development.
- BBC Sport is the first client to use the system.
- The technology can be sold to third parties for sports broadcasting, entertainment, and game shows.
- The system tracks players and ball movement, allowing viewers to see precisely how the ball was thrown.
- More cameras using Piero provide better detail.
- Piero will allow BBC to tell the story and passage of play.
