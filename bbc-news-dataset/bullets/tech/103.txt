- Report predicts nearly 70% of mobile phones sold will have a built-in camera by 2008.
- By 2008, Gartner predicts that 95% of all mobiles sold in Japan and Asia Pacific will have cameras.
- By mid-2005, the image resolution of most camera phones is likely to be more than two megapixels.
- In Europe, cameras on mobiles can take 1.3-megapixel images.
- Manufacturers are introducing design features like rotating screens, removable memory cards, and easier controls.
