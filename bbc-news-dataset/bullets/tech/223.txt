- Respond provides maps to aid groups via the web and on compact disc.
- Respond produces detailed maps for road networks, rivers, and villages for planning.
- Respond uses satellite imagery to produce accurate, rapidly usable maps.
- Rapid imaging allows aid groups to navigate wadis and mark washed-out roads.
- Maps are printed, laminated, and encapsulated for protection in the field.
- Respond is a European consortium providing up-to-date maps.
- Satellite technology and on-the-ground observation help keep relief flowing to those in need.
- Humanitarian groups in Darfur are using Respond's maps.
- Respond transfers data into information that non-technical people can easily understand.
