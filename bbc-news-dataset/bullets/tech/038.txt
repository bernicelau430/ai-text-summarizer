- The Motion Picture Association of America (MPAA) has been filing lawsuits against operators of BitTorrent server sites.
- The MPAA has filed 100 lawsuits since December.
- BitTorrent server sites do not host the files being shared.
- The MPAA has given operators that host eDonkey servers "take down" notices.
- The MPAA also succeeded in getting a network called LokiTorrent closed down after reaching a settlement.
