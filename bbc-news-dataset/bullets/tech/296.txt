- The explosion in consumer technology is to continue into 2005.
- The Consumer Electronics Show (CES) featured the pick of 2005's products.
- Portable technologies reflected one of the buzzwords of CES, which was the "time and place shifting" of multimedia content.
- There was a lack of exposure of Sony's new portable games device, the PSP, at the show.
- New ways of letting people take TV shows recorded on personal video recorders and watch them back on portable devices were unveiled.

- Hybrid devices, which combine a number of multimedia functions, were also in evidence on the show floor.
- Everything from tiny wearable MP3 players to giant high-definition TVs were showcased.
- More than 57% of the consumer electronics market is made up of female buyers.
