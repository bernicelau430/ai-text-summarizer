- Video game giant Electronic Arts (EA) says it wants to become the biggest entertainment firm in the world.
- EA said the video game industry was now bigger than the music industry.
- EA was planning an international awards show "similar to the Oscars and the Grammys" which would combine video games, music and movies.
- EA is in advanced stages of discussions over a reality TV show in which viewers could control the actions of the characters as in its popular game The Sims.
