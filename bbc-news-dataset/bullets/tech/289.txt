- Cellnet and Vodafone were the only mobile phone operators in the UK until 1993.
- Vodafone was the only firm with a mobile network for the first nine days of 1985.
- The first mobile phone call in the UK was made on the Vodafone network on January 1, 1985, by Ernie Wise.
- Mobile phones in the UK are celebrating their 20th anniversary.
- In the 20 years since that day, mobile phones have become an integral part of modern life and now almost 90% of Britons own a handset.
- Vodafone now has 14m customers in the UK alone.
