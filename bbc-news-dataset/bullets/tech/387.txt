- By early 2005, the net could have two new domain names.
- Names are 2 of 10 proposed domains being considered by Icann.
- In 2000, Icann approved 7 other new domains with varying degrees of success.
- Other proposed names include domains for pornography, Asia, mobile phones, anti-spam, and Catalan language/culture.
- Icann said its decision on the two domains was in response to detailed technical and commercial information.
- Despite approval, Icann cautioned there was no guarantee the domains would go into service.
- There are over 200 domain names, most for nations.
- The .post and .travel net domains have preliminary approval.
