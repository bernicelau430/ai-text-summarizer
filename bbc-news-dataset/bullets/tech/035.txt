- Iranian journalist Mr. Sigarchi was sentenced after an online campaign highlighted his case.
- Reporters Without Borders criticized the sentence as "harsh".
- Mr. Sigarchi also edits a newspaper in northern Iran.
- He gave interviews to BBC Persian Service and US-funded Radio Farda before his arrest.
- Iranian authorities have arrested around 20 online journalists during the crackdown.
- Around 10,000 people visited the campaign's website during the day.
