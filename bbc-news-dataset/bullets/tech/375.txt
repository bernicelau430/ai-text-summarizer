- In London, over 63,000 mobile phones have been left in black cabs in the past six months.
- That averages to about three phones per cab.
- Danes are seven times more likely to lose their mobile phones in cabs than travelers in Germany, Norway, and Sweden.
- More than twice as many laptops were left in London cabs compared to nine other surveyed cities.
- Londoners are the best at losing laptops in cabs.
- The Mobile Equipment National Database allows registration of phone IDs for return if lost or stolen.
- Firms like Retrofone offer cheap old-fashioned phones to replace lost expensive ones.
- Few people back up data on their phones.
