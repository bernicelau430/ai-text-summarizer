- The Mac Mini is small and has an inspired Apple design.
- It brings some of the best software packages within reach of more consumers than ever before.
- The Mac Mini is just a computer.
- It comes bundled with Mac OS X and iLife 05, a suite of software which includes iTunes, Safari, iPhoto, Garage Band and iDVD.
- The design is further evidence of a future when PCs are more than just bland, bulky boxes.
- Some users have adapted the Mac Mini to media center uses.
- One user has created a dock to enable him to plug in and out his Mac Mini in his car.
- Another user has mounted his Mac Mini to the back of his large plasma screen and controls the computer via a wireless keyboard and mouse.
- The Mac Mini is an ideal first computer or companion to a main computer for people who want to do interesting things with their music, photos and home movies.
- As a media centre computer, 80GB just seems too small.
- It is not a fully fledged revolution - it is a mini revolution.
- It is revolutionary in its size and looks, and the fact that it's the first Mac designed to really go for the low-cost PC market.
