- ScanSoft and Gracenote are developing technology for voice control of film and music libraries
- The technology will give people hands-free access to digital music and films
- Users will be able to request music that fits a mood or an occasion
- The firms gave no indication about whether the iPod, or any other media player, were in mind for the use of the voice control technology
- Portable players are becoming popular in cars, and auto firms are working with Apple to devise interfaces to control the firm's iPod music player
