- Twenty-eight people have been arrested in a global operation against Shadowcrew.com.
- The website was allegedly involved in identity fraud and credit card fraud.
- Those arrested are accused of trafficking counterfeit credit cards and false identification documents.
- The websites shared tips on how to commit fraud and provided a forum to buy information and tools.
- A 19-year-old man from Camberley, Surrey, was arrested but has been bailed.
- The US Secret Service was first tipped off in July 2003.
