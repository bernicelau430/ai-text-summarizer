- A report predicts that nearly 70% of mobile phones sold will have a camera by 2008.
- Image sensor technology in camera phones is improving.
- By 2008, 95% of mobiles sold in Japan and Asia Pacific will have cameras.
- By mid-2005, most camera phones will have image resolution of more than two megapixels.
- Four times more mobiles with cameras will be sold in Europe by the end of 2004 than last year.
