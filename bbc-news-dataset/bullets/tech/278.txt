- Online photo storage and sharing services are becoming popular.
- Many people's hard drives are full of photos.
- Photo giants like Kodak offer website storage, online editing, and print-ordering services.
- Services allow users to store and share pictures with family and friends.
- As file sizes increase, storage becomes a problem.
- Google released free software, Picasa, for organizing and finding digital photos.
- Picasa automatically detects photos as they are added to a PC.
- Some services, like Kodak's Ofoto and Snapfish, offer unlimited storage but require print purchases.
