- Windows cash machines have been around for several years
- Viruses affecting the network of the bank that owns them have caused cash machines to be unavailable for hours
- Move to Windows in cash machines was not without risks
- Banks start using Windows cash machines they also change the networking technology used to link the devices to their back office computers
- 70% of new cash machines being installed were Windows based
- A cash machine has a lifetime of up to 10 years
- Only about 10% of all ATMs get swapped for a newer model every year.
