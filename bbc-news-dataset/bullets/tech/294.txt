- ScanSoft and Gracenote are developing voice control technology for digital media players
- Users will be able to control their digital media players just by speaking to them
- Users will also be able to request music or films by actor's name
- No indication given if iPod or other players are in mind for the voice control technology
- Auto firms working with Apple to devise interfaces to control the firm's iPod music player
- The goal is to give people hands-free access to digital music and films in the car, at home, or on the move
