- A storage system has been incorporated into 3ami's application to log every computer action.
- Employers have a legal requirement to store data.
- The system is a joint venture between 3ami and BridgeHead Software.
- The system was developed due to the Freedom of Information Act.
- BridgeHead Software has techniques to prove file integrity legally.
- The system monitors computer activity, stores it, and retrieves disputed files quickly.
- The system is claimed to be a break-through in data monitoring and storage.
- The software monitors keystrokes and actions and can be used as legally binding evidence.
