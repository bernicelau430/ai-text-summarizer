- Banks must look after their databases properly.
- Criminal gangs may be applying for jobs which give them access to confidential customer data.
- Online bank Cahoot admitted that its customer account details could be read by anyone who could guess a login name.
- Banks should have good enough records and logs to trace the people who might have accessed the account details.
- Organizations that process personal data must take proper care of it, as defined in the Data Protection Act.
- Stringent data protection law, and civil lawsuits are two options that ensure companies improve their internal processes.
- Banks should take necessary measures to protect consumers from identity theft.
