- BT reports more broadband sign-ups in the last three months than any other quarter
- The 600,000 new connections bring BT's total to almost 3.3 million
- The numbers include those getting service directly from BT or via resellers
- More than 5 million people in the UK browse the net via broadband
- There are over 300 CPS firms, and BT lines for voice calls have shrunk to 55.4%
- The UK has 7.5 broadband connections per 100 people, compared to 6.7 in Germany and 15.8 in the Netherlands
- BT Retail provided about 30% of new broadband customers
- The surge was due to BT extending ADSL reach beyond 6km
