- The Motorsport Endeavour Club aims to involve disabled people in all areas of motorsport.
- Roy Tansley developed an electronic sequential gear changer after a leg amputation.
- Tansley's invention, the Pro-Shift, works with Hewland gearboxes.
- The Pro-Shift saves at least 20 seconds per lap in the Isle of Man TT.
- A Subaru Impreza rally car is adapted to accommodate various disabilities.
- The car is fitted with a 'duck clutch' instead of a clutch pedal.
- Customers have included amputees, quadriplegics, and stroke patients.
