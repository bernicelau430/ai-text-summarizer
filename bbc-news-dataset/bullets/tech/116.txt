- New strains of the Cabir mobile phone virus use short-range radio technology to spread to vulnerable phones.
- The Cabir virus only affects high-end handsets running the Symbian Series 60 phone operating system.
- A mistake in the original Cabir meant it could only infect one phone at a time.
- The new Cabir strains have corrected this mistake.
- There are no reports of any phones being infected by the new variants of Cabir.
