- The 600,000 connections take the total number of people in the UK signing up for broadband from BT to almost 3.3 million.
- Numbers include those that get service direct from BT or via re-sellers.
- More people signed up for broadband in the last three months than in any other quarter.
- UK total of 5.3 million translates into 7.5 connections per 100 people, compared to 6.7 in Germany and 15.8 in the Netherlands.
- BT Retail had a good quarter and provided about 30% of the new broadband customers.
- Surge due to BT stretching ADSL beyond 6km.
- More than 5 million browse the net via broadband.
