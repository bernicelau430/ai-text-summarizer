- Launched to encourage disabled people to get involved in all aspects of motorsport.
- Motorcycle racer, Roy Tansley, from Derby developed his electronic sequential gear changer following an accident which resulted in part of his left leg being amputated.
- Another exhibit on the Motorsport Endeavour stand is a Subaru Impreza rally car, adapted to accommodate a variety of disabilities.
- Tansley's invention, the Pro-Shift, is designed to work with Hewland gearboxes which are widely used in motorcycle racing.
- Tansley reckons that the Pro-Shift saves at least 20 seconds per lap when he competes in the Isle of Man TT.
