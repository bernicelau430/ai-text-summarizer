- Jez San, founder of Argonaut games group, has bought back most of the company after it went into administration.
- He has taken over Just Add Monsters studios and the London subsidiary Morpheme.
- Argonaut went into administration due to a cash crisis and fired about half of its staff.
- San is a key figure in the UK's games industry.
- The administrators are in talks over the sale of the Argonaut software division in Edgware.
