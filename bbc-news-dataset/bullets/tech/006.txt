- The Agricultural Information Project helps farmers in the Chancay-Huaral Valley in Peru.
- It uses a network of community computer centers linked by wireless technology.
- The Agricultural Information System, with its huaral.org website, is a key element.
- Farmers can share experiences with others in Peru and around the world.
- The network helps organize irrigation systems, which are vital in Peru's coastal areas.
- The Board of Irrigation Users aims to make the network self-sustainable through internet cafe revenue.
- The project includes training on computers and internet skills.
- The project was led by Cepes and backed by local institutions and European organizations.
