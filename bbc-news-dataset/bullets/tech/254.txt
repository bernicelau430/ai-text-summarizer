- Organisation launched to encourage disabled people to get involved in all aspects of motorsport.
- Technological innovations making it increasingly possible.
- Electronic sequential gear changer developed by motorcycle racer Roy Tansley.
- Pro-Shift works with Hewland gearboxes, widely used in motorcycle racing.
- Tansley reckons Pro-Shift saves at least 20 seconds per lap.
- Subaru Impreza rally car adapted to accommodate a variety of disabilities.
- Car fitted with 'duck clutch' - switch on gear stick used instead of clutch pedal.
- Customers have included right or left arm amputees, quadriplegics, stroke victims, and a woman who had all four limbs amputated.
