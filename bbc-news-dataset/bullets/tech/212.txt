- Ericsson interviewed 14,000 mobile phone owners on the ways they use their phone.
- People's habits remain the same.
- Early reports of camera phone usage in Japan seemed to imply that the innovation was going to be a flop, now 45% use it once a month.
- People also used their camera phones in very different ways to film and even digital cameras.
- Camera phones were being used much more to capture a moment and were being woven into everyday life.
- A mobile phone helps them keep it in a different form.
- Pioneer and materialists groups were most interested in trying new things and were behind the start of many trends in phone use.
