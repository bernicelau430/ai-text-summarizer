- Commodore was one of the pioneers in the chronology of home computing.
- The Commodore 64, launched in 1982, was one of the first affordable home PCs.
- The Commodore 64 sold more than any other single computer system.
- Commodore International filed for bankruptcy in 1994 and was sold to Tulip Computers.
- The Commodore computer brand could be resurrected after being bought by a US-based digital music distributor.
- In the late 1980s the firm was a great rival to Atari.
