- Surfers outside the US have been unable to visit the official re-election site of President George W Bush
- Netcraft monitoring stations in London, Amsterdam, and Sydney failed to view the site
- US monitoring stations could view the site without problems
- The George W Bush website began using Akamai's services on 21 October to ensure content delivery
- The site can still be accessed using anonymous proxy services based in the US
- The blocking of browsers outside the US began on Monday morning
