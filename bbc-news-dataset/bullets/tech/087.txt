- A US airline attendant is fighting for her job after being suspended over postings on her blog.
- Ms Simonetti was suspended on 25 September pending an investigation and has since lodged a complaint with the US Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC).
- She said her suspension has caused two of her friends to discontinue their blogs.
- As far as Ms Simonetti knows, there is no company anti-blogging policy.
