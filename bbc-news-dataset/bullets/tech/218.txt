- Commodore was one of the pioneers in home computing
- The Commodore 64, launched in 1982, was one of the first affordable home PCs
- The Commodore 64 sold more than any other single computer system to this day
- Commodore International filed for bankruptcy in 1994
- The Commodore brand could be resurrected after being bought by a US-based digital music distributor
- The company was a rival to Atari in the late 1980s
