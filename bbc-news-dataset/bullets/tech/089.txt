- Cellnet and Vodafone were the only mobile phone operators in the UK until 1993.
- Vodafone was the only firm with a mobile network in the UK for the first nine days of 1985.
- Britain's first mobile phone call was made across the Vodafone network on 1 January 1985 by Ernie Wise.
- Mobile phones in the UK are celebrating their 20th anniversary this weekend.
