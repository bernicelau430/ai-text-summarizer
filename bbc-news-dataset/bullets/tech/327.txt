- One in 10 adult Americans (22 million people) owns an MP3 player.
- MP3 players are the gadget of choice among affluent young Americans.
- Of the 22 million American MP3 player owners, 59% are men and 41% are women.
- MP3 players are becoming a mainstream technology for consumers.
- The American love affair with digital music players has been made possible as more and more homes get broadband.
