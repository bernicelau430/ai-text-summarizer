- Football Manager 2005 is the spiritual successor to the CM series.
- SI kept the game's code and database, and Eidos retained rights to the CM brand, and the look and feel of the game.
- In fact just about every area of the game has been tweaked, and it leads to an ever more immersive experience.
- Football Manager 2005 is out now for the PC and the Mac
- It is a realistic and satisfying football management game.
