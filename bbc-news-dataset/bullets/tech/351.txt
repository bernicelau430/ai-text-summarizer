- BT reports more broadband sign-ups in the last three months than any other quarter.
- 600,000 connections take the total number of people in the UK signing up for broadband from BT to almost 3.3 million.
- UK now has 5.3 million broadband users, 7.5 connections per 100 people.
- BT Retail provided about 30% of the new broadband customers.
- BT stretching the reach of ADSL - the UK's most widely used way of getting broadband - beyond 6km.
