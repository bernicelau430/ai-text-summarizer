- Surfers outside the US have been unable to visit the official re-election site of President George W Bush.
- Attempts to view the site through monitoring stations in London, Amsterdam, and Sydney failed.
- Since the early hours of 25 October, there has been failure of access.
- Netcraft's monitoring stations in the US managed to view the site with no problems.
- On 21 October, the George W Bush website began using Akamai.
- The site can still be seen using anonymous proxy services that are based in the US.
- The blocking of browsers outside the US began in the early hours of Monday morning.
