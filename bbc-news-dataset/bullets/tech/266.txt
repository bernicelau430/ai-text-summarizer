- The original Blinx aimed to attract platform game lovers to Xbox.
- Players can control time, while pigs control space.
- Players create two cat and two pig characters instead of playing as Blinx.
- Puzzles require time control, while pigs create warps, space bubbles, and void traps.
- Factions compete to recover pieces of a missing Time Crystal.
- Players swap between pig and feline factions.
- Time and space are controlled through VCR-style icons.
