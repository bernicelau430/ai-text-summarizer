- A new variant of the Bagle virus (Bagle.AT, Bagle.BB, and Bagle.AU) is spreading.
- The virus grabs e-mail addresses from Microsoft Outlook and sends itself to new victims.
- It also tries to turn off the firewall and security center services on Windows XP machines.
- The virus can strike computers running Windows 95, 98, ME, NT, 2000, and XP.
- Security firm Network Box stopped more than 30,000 copies an hour of the virus at its peak.
