- 600,000 new connections bring BT's UK broadband total to almost 3.3 million
- This includes those getting service directly from BT and through companies reselling BT lines
- More people signed up for broadband in the last three months than in any other quarter
- Over 5 million in the UK browse the net via broadband
- Part of the surge was due to BT extending ADSL reach beyond 6km
- There are over 300 firms offering CPS services and the percentage of people using BT lines for voice calls has shrunk to 55.4%
