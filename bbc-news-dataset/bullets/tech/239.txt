- The internet may get two new domain names: .mobi and .jobs.
- .mobi would be for websites and services designed for mobile phones.
- .jobs could be used by companies for dedicated job posting sites.
- The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (Icann) has given preliminary approval.
- The process to launch the new domain names could take months, and there are no guarantees of acceptance.
- They are among 10 new names being considered by Icann.
