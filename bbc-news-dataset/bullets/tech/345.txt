- Surfers outside the US have been unable to visit the official re-election site of President George W Bush.
- Since October 25, attempts to view the site from London, Amsterdam, and Sydney have failed.
- The site can still be seen using anonymous proxy services based in the US.
- On October 21, the George W Bush website began using Akamai to ensure content reaches visitors.
- The blocking of browsers outside the US began in the early hours of Monday morning.
