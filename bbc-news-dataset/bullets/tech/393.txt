- The Motorsport Endeavour Club launched to encourage disabled people in motorsport.
- Technologies on display adapt vehicles for various disabilities.
- Roy Tansley developed an electronic sequential gear changer after leg amputation.
- The Pro-Shift invention works with Hewland gearboxes used in motorcycle racing.
- In competition, the Pro-Shift saves at least 20 seconds per lap.
- A Subaru Impreza rally car is adapted to accommodate various disabilities.
- The car is fitted with a 'duck clutch' on the gear stick instead of a clutch pedal.
- Customers with disabilities have included arm amputees, quadriplegics, stroke survivors, and those with all four limbs amputated.
