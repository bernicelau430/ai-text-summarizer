- A technology would allow digital people and other computer-generated elements to be combined with the actual view seen by tourists as they walk around a historical site.
- The camera captures the view and feeds it to software on the computer.
- Crucial to the technique is the software that interprets the visitor's view.
- It would allow visitors a glimpse of life as it was originally lived in places such as Pompeii.
- The Lifeplus project is about combining digital and real views.
- The visitor would not just see the frescos, taverns and villas that have been excavated, but also people going about their daily life.
