- Speegle is a search engine with text-to-speech capabilities.
- It resembles Google in appearance but has no affiliation with it.
- Bright colors are used to aid visually impaired users.
- Experts question the real benefit for the visually impaired.
- RNIB is evaluating the technology.
- 80% of surfers use search engines as their first online action.
- RNIB expresses doubt over Speegle's added value for blind users.
